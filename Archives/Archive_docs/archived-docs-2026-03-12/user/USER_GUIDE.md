## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   ```
   Click "Sign Up" on homepage
   ```

2. **Fill in Your Details:**
   - Username (unique, 3-20 characters)
   - Email address (for verification)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Accept terms of service

3. **Verify Your Email:**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click verification link
   - You're ready to go!

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. **Navigate to Profile Settings**
   ```
   Click your avatar → Settings → Profile
   ```

2. **Complete Your Profile:**
   - **Profile Picture:** Upload a photo (max 5MB)
   - **Cover Photo:** Add a banner image
   - **Bio:** Tell others about yourself (max 500 characters)
   - **Location:** City/country (optional)
   - **Website:** Your personal website or social links
   - **Birthday:** For age verification (private)

3. **Privacy Settings:**
   - Choose who can see your profile
   - Control who can message you
   - Set post visibility defaults
   - Manage notification preferences

### Understanding the Interface

**Main Navigation:**
```
🏠 Home          - Your personalized feed
🔔 Notifications - Activity updates
✉️  Messages      - Direct messaging
👤 Profile       - Your profile page
🛍️  Shop          - E-commerce marketplace
📺 Media         - Videos, radio, TV
🤝 Collaborate   - Docs, projects, wikis
🔍 Search        - Find anything
```

**Quick Actions:**
- **Create Post:** Share button in navigation
- **Start Conversation:** Messages → New Conversation
- **Upload Media:** Media uploader in editor
- **Create Document:** Collaborate → New Document

## Social Features

### The Feed

Your personalized feed shows posts from people you follow and communities you've joined.

#### Viewing Your Feed

**Feed Types:**
1. **Home Feed:** Mix of everything you're subscribed to
2. **Trending:** Popular posts right now
3. **Following:** Only people you follow
4. **Communities:** Posts from your communities

**Sorting Options:**
- **Hot:** Trending content (default)
- **New:** Latest first
- **Top:** Most engaged content  
- **Rising:** Gaining popularity fast
- **Controversial:** Highly debated

#### Creating Posts

**Post Types:**

1. **Text Post:**
   ```
   Click "What's on your mind?"
   Type your message (up to 280 characters for quick posts)
   Click "Post"
   ```

2. **Image/Video Post:**
   ```
   Click camera icon
   Upload media (images up to 10MB, videos up to 100MB)
   Add caption
   Click "Post"
   ```

3. **Link Post:**
   ```
   Paste a URL
   Platform auto-generates preview
   Add your comment
   Click "Post"
   ```

4. **Poll Post:**
   ```
   Click "Create Poll"
   Add question + options (2-4 choices)
   Set duration (1 hour to 7 days)
   Click "Post"
   ```

**Post Privacy:**
- 🌍 **Public:** Anyone can see
- 👥 **Friends:** Only friends can see
- 🔒 **Private:** Only you can see
- 👫 **Custom:** Select specific people

**Hashtags & Mentions:**
```
Use #hashtags to categorize posts
Use @username to mention people
Example: "Just launched my new project! #coding #webdev @john"
```

#### Engaging with Posts

**Reactions:**
- 👍 Like
- ❤️ Love

- 😮 Wow
- 😢 Sad
- 😠 Angry

*Hold down like button to choose reaction*

**Actions:**
- **Comment:** Share your thoughts
- **Share:** Repost to your feed
- **Quote:** Share with your comment
- **Bookmark:** Save for later

**Comments:**
- Reply to posts

- React to comments

## Tips & Best Practices

### Creating Engaging Posts

✅ **Do:**
- Use clear, compelling images
- Write engaging headlines
- Use relevant hashtags (3-5)
- Post at optimal times
- Engage with comments
- Be authentic and genuine

❌ **Don't:**
- Spam or post too frequently
- Use clickbait
- Over-hashtag (#stop #using #20 #hashtags)
- Post low-quality content
- Engage in arguments
- Share fake news

### Building Your Following

1. **Post consistently** (daily or few times per week)
2. **Engage with others** (comment, like, share)
3. **Use hashtags** strategically
4. **Join communities** in your niche
5. **Collaborate** with others
6. **Be helpful** and add value
7. **Complete your profile** fully

### Staying Safe Online

1. **Use strong, unique password**
2. **Enable 2FA**
3. **Don't share personal info** (address, phone, ID, etc.)
4. **Be cautious with links** (phishing)
5. **Report suspicious activity**
6. **Don't send money** to strangers
7. **Think before you post** (it's public!)

## FAQ

**Q: Is Let's Connect free?**
A: Yes! Core features are completely free. Premium features may be added in the future.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Verification (checkmark badge) is available for public figures, brands, and organizations. Submit a verification request with proof of identity.

**Q: Can I use Let's Connect for business?**
A: Absolutely! Create a Page for your business, list products in Shop, and connect with customers.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Click the "Report" button on any post, comment, or message. Select a reason and submit.

**Q: Can I delete my messages?**
A: Yes, you can delete messages you've sent. Note: Recipient may have already seen them.

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: Images (JPG, PNG, GIF), Videos (MP4, WebM), Documents (PDF, DOCX), Audio (MP3, WAV)

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: We respect your privacy. See our [Privacy Policy] for details. You control your data and can download or delete it anytime.
