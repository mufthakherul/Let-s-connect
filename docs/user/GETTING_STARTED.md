## Creating Your Account

Getting started on Milonexa begins with account creation. Follow these steps to register:

### Step 1: Access the Registration Page

1. Open Milonexa in your web browser
2. Look for the **"Sign Up"** button on the landing page (typically in the top-right corner)
3. Click it to access the registration form

### Step 2: Fill Out Registration Form

The registration form requires the following information:

**Username**
- Must be between 3-30 characters
- Can contain letters, numbers, and underscores
- Once set, you can change it later in your settings (up to once per 30 days)
- This is your unique identifier on the platform

**Email Address**
- Must be a valid, active email address
- You'll receive a verification code here
- Can be changed later in account settings
- We recommend using an email you check regularly

**Password**
- Minimum 8 characters required
- Must contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Must contain at least one number (0-9)
- Must contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- Choose something unique that you don't use elsewhere

**Display Name**
- This is the name shown to other users on your profile
- Can be different from your username
- Examples: "John Smith", "Creative Designer", "Tech Enthusiast"
- Can contain spaces and special characters
- Change it anytime in your profile settings

**Date of Birth**
- Required for age verification (minimum age is 13)
- Used to determine age-appropriate content recommendations
- Kept private unless you choose to display it on your profile
- Format: MM/DD/YYYY

### Step 3: Accept Terms and Privacy Policy

Before completing registration:
- Review the Terms of Service by clicking the link provided
- Review the Privacy Policy to understand how we handle your data
- Check the box confirming you accept both documents
- You must accept these to continue

### Step 4: Complete Registration

1. Click the **"Create Account"** button
2. You'll see a confirmation message
3. Check your email for the verification code (see next section)

**Note:** If you don't see the confirmation email within 2 minutes, check your spam folder. If it's still missing, you can request a new code during the email verification step.

## Email Verification

After registration, you'll need to verify your email address to activate your account.

### Verification Process

**What You'll See:**
1. After registration, you're taken to the verification page
2. A message appears: "We've sent a 6-digit verification code to your@email.com"
3. You'll see input fields for a 6-digit code

**Checking Your Email:**
1. Open the email from Milonexa with subject "Verify your Milonexa account"
2. The email contains a 6-digit code (example: 123456)
3. Copy this code carefully

**Entering the Code:**
1. Return to the Milonexa verification page
2. Enter the 6-digit code in the provided fields
3. Click **"Verify"** or press Enter
4. The code is valid for 10 minutes

**Success!**
- Your email is now verified
- You're logged in and ready to complete your profile
- You'll be redirected to the profile completion page

### If You Don't Receive the Code

**Resend the Code:**
1. On the verification page, look for **"Didn't receive a code?"** link
2. Click **"Resend Code"**
3. A new 6-digit code will be sent to your email immediately
4. You can resend up to 5 times per hour

**Still Not Receiving It?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify the email address was entered correctly during registration
- Check if your email provider is blocking Milonexa emails
- Wait 2-3 minutes before checking (email delivery can take time)
- Contact support@milonexa.com if problems persist

**Expired Code:**
- Codes expire after 10 minutes
- If your code expired, use the "Resend Code" option
- There's no penalty for expired codes; just request a new one

## Completing Your Profile

Once your email is verified, complete your profile to enhance your Milonexa experience.

### Profile Picture (Avatar)

**Why Upload an Avatar:**
- Helps friends recognize you
- Makes your profile more inviting
- Required for some features (like video calls)
- Displays across the entire platform

**How to Upload:**
1. Click the **"Upload Avatar"** button or the camera icon
2. Select from your computer:
   - **Accepted formats:** JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
   - **Maximum file size:** 5MB
   - **Recommended size:** 400x400 pixels (at least 100x100)
3. Crop the image if needed using the provided tool
4. Click **"Save"** or **"Confirm"**

**Tips for a Great Avatar:**
- Use a clear, well-lit photo of your face
- Avoid blurry or overly dark images
- Ensure your face is centered and visible
- Professional headshots work great
- For branded accounts, use your logo

### Cover Photo

**Adding a Cover Photo:**
1. Scroll down on the profile completion page
2. Click **"Upload Cover Photo"** or the banner upload area
3. Select an image from your computer:
   - **Accepted formats:** JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
   - **Maximum file size:** 10MB
   - **Recommended size:** 1200x400 pixels
4. Adjust positioning if needed
5. Click **"Save"**

**Cover Photo Ideas:**
- Scenic backgrounds related to your interests
- Your brand colors for professional accounts
- Inspiring quotes overlaid on images
- Photos from your travels or hobbies

### Bio

**Writing Your Bio:**
1. Click the bio text field
2. Write a brief description about yourself
3. Maximum 500 characters
4. Include your interests, profession, or personality traits

**Bio Tips:**
- Be genuine and authentic
- Keep it concise but informative
- Use emojis to add personality ✨
- Update it regularly to reflect current interests
- Include relevant keywords (helps with search)

**Example Bios:**
- "🎨 Graphic Designer | Coffee Lover ☕ | Based in NYC"
- "Tech enthusiast exploring AI and automation. Always learning!"
- "Photographer 📸 | Travel addict 🌍 | Sharing beautiful moments"

### Location

**Setting Your Location:**
1. Click the location field
2. Type your city and country
3. Select from the dropdown suggestions
4. You can set this to public or keep it private in settings

**Why Add Location:**
- Helps connect with people nearby
- Local event recommendations
- Shows on your profile if you choose to display it
- Used for location-based features (coming soon)

### Website URL

**Adding Your Website:**
1. Click the website field
2. Enter a full URL (include http:// or https://)
3. Examples: "https://yourportfolio.com" or "https://yourstore.com"
4. Click outside the field to save

**Valid URLs:**
- Personal portfolio or resume website
- Business website
- Blog URL
- Social media profile links
- YouTube channel

### Complete Your Profile

1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Click **"Finish Setup"** or **"Complete Profile"**
3. You'll be taken to the homepage
4. Your profile is now live and visible to other users!

**Next Steps:**
- You can edit any profile information anytime in your settings
- Additional profile options (like interests, education) are available in detailed settings
- Start exploring and connecting!

## Making Your First Post

Share your thoughts with the Milonexa community through posts.

### Accessing the Post Composer

**Option 1: From the Feed**
1. Click "What's on your mind?" at the top of the feed
2. The post composer opens in a modal or expands on the page

**Option 2: From Your Profile**
1. Go to your profile
2. Click "Create a Post" button

**Option 3: Quick Post**
1. Press 'P' (keyboard shortcut) from any page
2. The composer opens instantly

### Writing Your Post

**Text Content:**
1. Click in the text area
2. Type your message (no character limit for posts)
3. Format text:
   - **Bold:** Use **text** or select and press Ctrl+B (Cmd+B on Mac)
   - _Italic:_ Use _text_ or select and press Ctrl+I (Cmd+I)
   - `Code:` Use `text` for inline code
   - Links: Paste URLs directly or use [text](url) format

**Adding Media:**
1. Click the image/video icon
2. Upload up to 10 files per post
3. Drag and drop images to reorder
4. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, MP4, WebM
5. Maximum file size: 100MB per file

**Adding Tags:**
1. Type @ followed by a username to mention someone
2. Type # followed by a word to create/add to a hashtag
3. Mentioned users get notified
4. Hashtags improve discoverability

**Emoji and Reactions:**
1. Click the smiley face icon to open emoji picker
2. Search emoji by name
3. Click to add to post

### Setting Visibility

**Before posting, set who can see it:**

**Public** (Default)
- Everyone on Milonexa can see it
- Appears in search results
- Can be shared widely
- Recommended for general content

**Friends Only**
- Only people on your friend list can see it
- Won't appear in search results
- Still appears in group posts if shared there
- Good for personal updates

**Private/Just Me**
- Only you can see it
- Use for drafts or personal reminders
- Can be changed to public later

**Specific Groups**
- Only visible within selected groups
- Great for group discussions
- Doesn't appear elsewhere on the platform
- Group members receive notifications

**How to Set Visibility:**
1. Click the visibility dropdown (globe icon or "Public" text)
2. Select desired privacy level
3. Click outside dropdown to confirm
4. Icon shows your current setting

### Publishing Your Post

**Review Before Posting:**
1. Reread your text for typos
2. Check that media files uploaded correctly
3. Verify visibility setting is correct
4. Ensure mentions are accurate

**Publishing:**
1. Click **"Post"** or **"Share"** button
2. Processing message appears
3. Post appears at top of feed within seconds
4. You can edit or delete within 24 hours

**After Posting:**
- Post appears on your profile and followers' feeds
- You receive notifications for likes, comments, shares
- Monitor comments and respond to engagement
- Use analytics (if available) to see post performance

### Editing and Deleting

**Edit a Post:**
1. Hover over your post
2. Click the three-dot menu icon
3. Select "Edit"
4. Make changes and click "Save"
5. Edited posts show "edited" label and timestamp

**Delete a Post:**
1. Click the three-dot menu on your post
2. Select "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Post is removed permanently (30-day recovery period)

## Finding and Adding Friends

Connect with others on Milonexa to enhance your experience.

### Searching for Friends

**Using the Search Function:**
1. Click the Search icon in navigation
2. Type a person's name or username
3. Results show user profiles with:
   - Profile picture
   - Display name and username
   - Bio/location
   - Mutual friends count
   - Follow/Friend status

**Browsing Suggestions:**
1. Go to the Friends section
2. Scroll "Suggestions for You"
3. See recommended people based on:
   - Mutual friends
   - Shared interests
   - Groups you're both in
   - Similar activity

**Finding People in Groups:**
1. Go to a group
2. Click "Members" or "Browse Members"
3. View all group members
4. Click to view profiles and send friend requests

### Sending a Friend Request

**Steps:**
1. Open the person's profile
2. Look for **"Add Friend"** or **"Send Friend Request"** button
3. Click the button
4. Status changes to "Request Pending"
5. The person receives a notification

**What Happens Next:**
- Your request appears in their "Friend Requests" section
- They can accept or decline
- You can see pending requests in your Friends section
- Requests don't expire automatically

**What They See:**
- Notification: "[Your Name] sent you a friend request"
- Your profile and bio in their friend requests
- Option to accept or decline
- Can also be ignored (no response needed)

### Accepting Friend Requests

**When You Receive a Request:**
1. You get a notification
2. Click notification to view the profile
3. Or go to Friends → Pending Requests

**To Accept:**
1. View the request
2. Click **"Accept"** button
3. You're now friends
4. Their posts appear in your feed
5. You can message them

**To Decline:**
1. Click **"Decline"** or **"Ignore"** button
2. Request is removed
3. You can't see their public profile if they have privacy enabled
4. They don't know you declined (unless their settings show it)

### Managing Your Friends List

**Viewing Your Friends:**
1. Go to Friends section
2. See list of all your friends
3. Click any friend to view their profile
4. Chat, view their posts, see mutual connections

**Removing a Friend:**
1. Go to Friends or their profile
2. Click the friend status dropdown or menu
3. Select "Remove Friend"
4. Confirm action
5. Their posts no longer appear in your feed
6. You're removed from their friends list

**Blocking Someone:**
1. Go to their profile
2. Click menu (three dots)
3. Select "Block User"
4. They can't see your profile, posts, or message you
5. Unblock anytime in settings → Blocked Users

**Reporting Someone:**
1. Click the menu on their profile or post
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose reason: Harassment, spam, inappropriate content, etc.
4. Provide details and submit
5. Milonexa team reviews and takes action

## Joining a Group

Groups are communities centered around shared interests.

### Finding Groups

**Search for Groups:**
1. Go to the Groups section
2. Click **"Browse Groups"** or **"Explore"**
3. Search by name or topic
4. Filter by category, size, or activity level
5. View group preview showing members and posts

**Suggested Groups:**
1. When you visit Groups, see personalized suggestions
2. Based on your interests and friend activity
3. View member count and description
4. Join with one click

**From Friends:**
1. See what groups your friends joined
2. On their profiles or in group notifications
3. Easy to join and connect with friends

### Joining a Group

**For Public Groups:**
1. Open the group
2. Click **"Join Group"** button
3. Confirmation message appears
4. You're immediately a member
5. Start posting and participating

**For Private Groups:**
1. Open the group
2. Click **"Request to Join"** button
3. Join request sent to group admins
4. Wait for approval (usually within hours)
5. Notification when accepted

**What Changes After Joining:**
- Group appears in your Groups list
- Group posts appear in your feed
- You can post in the group
- You're in the member directory
- Notifications for group activity

### Participating in a Group

**Posting in a Group:**
1. Open the group
2. Click in the post area
3. Create your post
4. Set visibility to the group (default)
5. Post appears to all group members

**Group Features:**
- **Feed:** Latest posts from all members
- **Members:** Directory of all members with profiles
- **About:** Group description, rules, and purpose
- **Events:** Scheduled group events or meetups
- **Files:** Shared documents and media
- **Discussions:** Threaded conversations

**Etiquette Tips:**
- Read group rules first
- Keep posts on-topic
- Be respectful to all members
- Don't spam or self-promote excessively
- Report inappropriate behavior to moderators

### Leaving a Group

**Steps:**
1. Open the group
2. Click the settings/menu icon
3. Select **"Leave Group"**
4. Confirm action
5. Group removed from your list
6. You can rejoin anytime

**What Happens:**
- Your posts remain in the group
- Group posts no longer appear in your feed
- You're removed from member directory
- You won't receive group notifications
