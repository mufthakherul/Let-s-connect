## Viewing Your Profile

Your profile is the public-facing representation of your account and the main way other users learn about you.

### Accessing Your Profile

**Method 1: From Navigation**
1. Click your avatar or username in the main navigation
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Taken to your profile page

**Method 2: From Settings**
1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Click "View Profile" or "View Public Profile"
3. Opens your profile as others see it

**Method 3: Direct URL**
- Navigate to https://milonexa.com/@[your-username]
- Example: https://milonexa.com/@john_smith
- Works whether logged in or not

### Profile Components

**Header Section:**
- Cover photo (1200×400 pixels)
- Profile picture (circular, upper-left of cover)
- Verification badge (if verified account)
- Follow/Friend button (if another user's profile)

**About Section:**
- Display name
- Username (@username)
- Bio/description
- Location (if made public)
- Website link (if added)
- Join date
- Follower/Following counts

**Post Feed:**
- Your recent posts in chronological order
- Only shows posts with visibility set to public or applicable audience
- Filters available: All, With images, With videos

**Tabs:**
- **Posts:** Your public posts
- **Media:** Photos and videos you've shared
- **Likes:** Posts you've liked (if this tab is made public)
- **About:** More detailed profile information

### How Your Profile Appears to Others

**What Others See (Public):**
- Display name and username
- Profile picture and cover photo
- Bio and location
- Website link
- Public posts
- Follower/Following counts
- Join date
- Verification status

**What Others Don't See:**
- Email address (never shown publicly)
- Phone number
- Date of birth (unless you made it public)
- Private posts
- Archived posts
- Blocked users
- Bookmarks and saved posts

**Privacy Levels for Profile Visibility:**
- **Public:** Anyone on the internet can see your profile
- **Friends Only:** Only your friends can view profile
- **Private:** Requires following approval; only followers can see
- **Hidden:** Profile not visible; only you can view

## Editing Your Profile

Customize your profile to reflect your personality and interests.

### Accessing Profile Editor

**Steps:**
1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Or click the edit button (pencil icon) on your profile header
4. Profile editor opens with all editable fields

**Save Changes:**
- Click "Save" or "Save Changes" when done
- Success message appears: "Profile updated successfully"
- Changes visible on profile immediately
- Posted to activity feed (optional, configurable)

### Avatar (Profile Picture)

**Image Requirements:**
- **Formats:** JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Maximum size:** 5MB
- **Recommended dimensions:** 400×400 pixels (minimum 100×100)
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square) for best appearance
- **File name:** Any name is fine

**How to Upload:**
1. Click "Upload Avatar" or camera icon
2. Select "Choose File" or drag-and-drop image
3. Image preview appears
4. Adjust crop area if needed
   - Drag to reposition image
   - Pinch to zoom in/out
   - Corners show crop area
5. Click "Crop" or "Confirm"
6. New avatar previewed
7. Click "Save" to apply

**Avatar Tips:**
- Use high-quality, well-lit photo
- Face should be clearly visible
- Avoid extremely dark or blurry images
- Professional headshot recommended
- For brands, use your logo
- Update occasionally to stay current
- Animated GIFs work but may not preview smoothly

**Changing Your Avatar:**
1. Click avatar on profile editor
2. Follow upload process
3. Previous avatar replaced
4. Notification sent to followers (optional)

**Removing Your Avatar:**
1. Click avatar in editor
2. Select "Remove Avatar"
3. Default avatar appears
4. Default avatar shows initials or generic image

### Cover Photo

**Image Requirements:**
- **Formats:** JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Maximum size:** 10MB
- **Recommended dimensions:** 1200×400 pixels
- **Minimum dimensions:** 640×200 pixels
- **Aspect ratio:** 3:1 for best display

**How to Upload:**
1. Click "Upload Cover Photo" or banner area
2. Select image file or drag-and-drop
3. Preview displayed
4. Adjust positioning if needed
   - Drag to reposition
   - Zoom controls to adjust scale
5. Click "Set" or "Confirm"
6. Click "Save" to apply

**Cover Photo Ideas:**
- Scenic landscape or travel photo
- Professional background for business profile
- Branded colors and design
- Inspirational quote overlaid on image
- Hobby or interest related imagery
- Abstract art or patterns
- Seasonal or themed backgrounds

**Changing Cover Photo:**
1. Click on cover area
2. Upload new image
3. Adjust positioning
4. Confirm change

**Removing Cover Photo:**
1. Click cover area
2. Select "Remove"
3. Solid background appears

### Display Name

**Guidelines:**
- **Length:** 2-100 characters
- **Allowed:** Letters, numbers, spaces, hyphens, apostrophes, periods
- **NOT allowed:** @ symbols, # symbols, excessive punctuation
- **Examples:** "John Smith", "Dr. Jane Adams", "Maria García"

**How to Change:**
1. Click display name field in editor
2. Clear current name
3. Type new display name
4. See character count: "87/100 characters"
5. Click "Save"

**Display Name vs Username:**
- **Display name:** What friends see (can change anytime)
- **Username:** Unique identifier (can change once per 30 days)
- They can be completely different
- Display name is shown more prominently
- Username appears in profile URL

### Username

**Rules:**
- **Length:** 3-30 characters
- **Allowed:** Letters, numbers, underscores (_)
- **NOT allowed:** Spaces, special characters (except underscore)
- **Case:** Case-insensitive; "John_Smith" = "john_smith"
- **Start/end:** Cannot start with number or end with underscore

**Changing Username:**
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Click "Change Username"
3. Enter new username
4. Click "Check availability" to verify
5. If available, confirm
6. See confirmation: "Username changed to @newusername"

**Change Frequency:**
- Limited to once every 30 days for security
- Protects against account hijacking
- Countdown shown if recently changed
- Previous username becomes available for reuse

**Username Tips:**
- Choose username you're comfortable with long-term
- Avoid numbers at start
- Keep it memorable
- Professional usernames work best
- Avoid changing frequently (confuses followers)

- **Maximum length:** 500 characters

3. Type new bio

- Update when your situation changes

**Bio Features:**

  - Example: "Love working with @jane_smith on projects"

- **#Hashtags:** Type #topic to create/link to hashtag

## Profile Visibility

Control who can see your profile and how it appears to different people.

### Profile Visibility Levels

**Public Profile (Default)**
- Anyone on the internet can see your profile
- Appears in search results
- Accessible without login
- Shows up on anyone's profile who mentions you
- Recommended for public figures and content creators

**Friends Only**
- Only people on your friends list can view
- Doesn't appear in search results to non-friends
- Profile visible to other logged-in users if mutuals
- Private but accessible to established connections

**Private (Followers Approval)**
- People must request to follow you
- You approve/deny follow requests
- Only followers see your profile
- Doesn't appear in public search
- Best for personal accounts wanting control

**Hidden**
- Profile only visible to you
- Cannot be found in search
- Friends must use direct link to find you
- Other users see "This profile is hidden"
- Most private option

### Changing Profile Visibility

**Steps:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Find "Profile Visibility"
3. Select desired visibility level:
   - Public
   - Friends Only
   - Private
   - Hidden
4. Confirm selection
5. Changes apply immediately

**Effects of Changing:**
- **Public to Friends Only:** Non-friends lose access immediately
- **Any to Hidden:** Profile becomes unfindable
- **Private to Public:** All followers can see posts
- **Any to Private:** Followers must be re-approved

### Visibility by User Type

**If Your Profile is Public:**
- Search results show your profile
- Non-followers can view
- Posts visible based on post privacy settings
- Can receive messages from anyone
- Friend requests visible from anyone

**If Your Profile is Friends Only:**
- Only friends see profile
- Non-friends cannot browse your posts
- Posts remain private
- Friend requests come from anyone
- Can change visibility of past posts

**If Your Profile is Private:**
- Users must request to follow
- You control who follows
- Non-followers cannot see profile
- Posts limited to followers
- Message requests go to a separate folder

**If Your Profile is Hidden:**
- Only you can see full profile
- Link sharing doesn't work
- Search results don't show you
- Messages won't be received publicly
- Followers can still access if they have link

## Privacy Settings

Comprehensive privacy controls for managing who can interact with you.

### Accessing Privacy Settings

1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Click "Privacy" in the settings menu
3. See all privacy configuration options
4. Make changes and click "Save"

### Post Visibility Controls

**Default Post Visibility:**
- **Public:** Everyone can see
- **Friends Only:** Only friends can see
- **Private:** Only you can see
- **Specific Groups:** Only visible in selected groups

**How to Set Default:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Find "Default Post Visibility"
3. Select preference
4. When you create posts, this is preset visibility
5. Can override per-post

**Can Others Share Your Posts:**
- Toggle "Allow shares of my posts"
- When ON: Anyone can repost/share
- When OFF: Only you can share your content
- Applies to all posts

**Can Others Comment:**
- Toggle "Allow comments on my posts"
- When ON: Anyone can comment (based on follower status)
- When OFF: Comments disabled on all posts
- Can override per-post

### Friend Request Settings

**Who Can Send Friend Requests:**
- **Everyone:** Anyone can request
- **Friends Only:** Only current friends can request (usually for adding new friends)
- **Nobody:** No one can request; must be added by you

**Pending Requests:**
- See in Friends > Pending Requests
- Accept, decline, or ignore requests
- Option to block sender

**Blocking Friend Requests:**
1. Go to Privacy Settings
2. Find "Block specific users"
3. Click "Block"
4. User cannot send requests
5. Cannot see your profile

### Messaging Controls

**Who Can Message You:**
- **Everyone:** Anyone can message
- **Friends Only:** Only friends can message
- **Nobody:** No one can message

**Message Request Folder:**
- Messages from non-friends go to separate folder
- You can accept, block, or report
- Don't have to respond

**Block Messaging:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Messaging
2. Choose "Friends Only" or "Nobody"
3. Others see message: "This user doesn't accept messages from you"
4. Can change anytime

### Search and Discoverability

**Can Others Find You:**
- Toggle "Make my profile searchable"
- When ON: Appear in search results
- When OFF: Only accessible via direct link

**Search Result Information:**
- If searchable, shows in username/name searches
- Location search may include you
- Interest searches may surface your profile
- Hashtag followers may find you

**Hide From Search:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Search
2. Toggle "Make my profile searchable" OFF
3. You still appear to friends
4. Not indexed by search engines
5. Reduces discoverability

### Connection-Based Visibility

**Who Sees What:**
- **Strangers (no connection):**
  - Only see public profile and public posts
  - Cannot message (if restricted)
  - Cannot see friend list (if restricted)

- **Friends:**
  - See full profile
  - See friends-only posts
  - Can message directly
  - See your full friend list (if enabled)

- **Followers (private profile):**
  - See full profile
  - See all shared posts
  - Can message if you allow
  - See selected portions of friend list

### Data Visibility Toggles

**Show Friend List:**
- Toggle "Make friend list visible"
- ON: Others see who you're friends with
- OFF: Friend list private
- Helps others find mutual friends

**Show Following List:**
- Toggle "Make following list visible"
- ON: Others see who you follow
- OFF: Following list private

**Show Liked Posts:**
- Toggle "Make liked posts visible"
- ON: Others see posts you've liked (if they have access to likes)
- OFF: Likes are private

**Show Bookmarks:**
- Toggle "Make bookmarks visible"
- ON: Followers can see your saved posts
- OFF: Bookmarks are completely private

### Activity Status

**Show When Online:**
- Toggle "Show activity status"
- ON: Others see when you're active
- OFF: Appear offline to all users
- Affects chat, profile, and general status

**Activity Timeout:**
- Set how long after last action you show as "away"
- Options: 5 min, 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour
- Longer = appear offline sooner
- Shorter = appear offline later

### Sensitive Content Controls

**Adult Content:**
- Toggle to show/hide mature content
- When OFF: Sensitive posts filtered
- When ON: See all content
- Age-based (18+ content requires toggle)

**Muting Content:**
- Can mute specific words, hashtags, topics
- When enabled, posts with those terms hidden
- Applied across feed, search, trends
- Configurable per word

**Content Filtering:**
- Choose content types to see
- Toggles for: Politics, News, Religion, Sports, etc.
- Affects feed recommendations
- Reduces unwanted content

## Security Settings

Protect your account with comprehensive security features.

### Accessing Security Settings

1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Click "Security" tab
3. View all security options
4. Configure protections

### Password Management

**Change Password:**
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter current password (required)
4. Enter new password:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, special char
5. Confirm new password by entering again
6. Click "Change Password"
7. Confirmation message appears
8. Logged out of all sessions except current

**Password Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter (A-Z)
- One lowercase letter (a-z)
- One number (0-9)
- One special character (!@#$%^&*-_=+)

**Password Tips:**
- Don't reuse previous 5 passwords
- Use phrase-based passwords for strength
- Enable password manager for secure storage
- Never share password with anyone
- Change if suspect compromise

### Active Sessions

**What Are Sessions:**
- Logged-in instances on devices
- Includes browser, mobile app, desktop app
- Multiple sessions allowed
- Each session has independent token

**View All Sessions:**
1. Go to Settings > Security > Active Sessions
2. See list of all active logins
3. Information shown:
   - Device type and browser
   - Operating system
   - Approximate location
   - Last activity time
   - IP address (partial, privacy-masked)

**Session Details:**
- Chrome on Windows 10 | New York, USA | Last active: Just now | 192.168.*.*
- Safari on iOS | San Francisco, USA | Last active: 2 hours ago | 203.*.*.* 
- Firefox on macOS | London, UK | Last active: 1 day ago | 185.*.*.* 

### Revoking Sessions

**Sign Out Individual Session:**
1. Go to Active Sessions list
2. Find session to end
3. Click "Sign Out" button
4. Confirm: "Sign out this session?"
5. Session terminated immediately
6. That device logs out
7. Token invalidated
8. Device must log in again to access

**Sign Out All Sessions:**
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Sign Out All Sessions" or "Sign Out Everywhere"
3. Confirmation: "You will be signed out everywhere except this device"
4. Confirm action
5. All other sessions terminated
6. Current session remains active
7. Others must log back in

**Use Cases for Revoke:**
- Unfamiliar device or location
- Suspect account compromise
- Lost or stolen device
- Changing passwords
- Security audit

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**2FA Status:**
- Shows current 2FA status: Enabled/Disabled
- If enabled, shows setup date

**Enable 2FA:**
1. Click "Enable 2FA"
2. Enter password to confirm
3. Choose authentication method:
   - Authenticator app (TOTP) - Recommended
   - SMS text message (if available)
4. Follow setup prompts
5. Save backup codes
6. 2FA active

**Manage Authenticator App:**
1. Go to Settings > Security > 2FA
2. See "Authenticator App" section
3. Options:
   - Change authenticator app
   - View setup key
   - Regenerate QR code
4. Follow setup process if changing app

**Backup Codes:**
- 10 emergency codes provided
- Each code single-use only
- For access if authenticator unavailable
- Store securely (password manager, safe)
- Never share or email

**Regenerate Backup Codes:**
1. Go to Settings > Security > 2FA
2. Click "Regenerate Codes"
3. Confirmation: "New codes will replace old codes"
4. Confirm action
5. Old codes invalid
6. New codes provided (save securely)

**Disable 2FA:**
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Disable 2FA"
3. Enter password to confirm
4. Confirmation warning
5. 2FA disabled
6. No longer needed for login

### Account Activity

**View Login History:**
1. Go to Settings > Security > Login History
2. See recent logins with:
   - Date and time
   - Device type and browser
   - Location
   - Success/failure status
   - IP address

**What's Tracked:**
- Successful logins
- Failed login attempts (if 3+ in an hour)
- Password changes
- 2FA configuration changes
- Session creations/terminations
- Privacy setting changes

**View Complete History:**
- Last 30 days shown by default
- Scroll to see more
- Filter by device type
- Filter by status (success/failure)
- Export history option

### Suspicious Activity Alerts

**Alert Types:**
1. **New Device Login Alert**
   - When you log in from new device
   - Email sent immediately
   - Shows device, location, time
   - Action: Review and confirm safe

2. **Multiple Failed Logins Alert**
   - When 10+ failed attempts in 1 hour
   - Email sent immediately
   - Shows location and time
   - Action: Change password immediately

3. **Unusual Access Alert**
   - Unusual location (far from normal)
   - Rapid location changes
   - Multiple simultaneous sessions
   - Email sent with details
   - Action: Review sessions and change password

4. **Account Settings Changed**
   - Email change attempted
   - Password changed
   - 2FA disabled
   - Privacy settings changed
   - Notification sent

**Alert Frequency:**
- Can be configured in Settings > Notifications
- Set to email you immediately, daily digest, or off
- Critical alerts always sent via email

### Account Recovery Options

**Recovery Email:**
- Add secondary email address
- Used if primary email compromised
- Set in Settings > Account > Recovery Options
- Receives password reset link if primary fails

**Recovery Phone Number:**
- Add phone number
- Used for SMS 2FA
- Receives account alerts
- SMS backup for password reset (if available)

**Security Questions:**
- Add backup recovery method
- Answer questions if email/phone unavailable
- Set in Settings > Security > Recovery Questions
- 3-5 questions to choose from

## Font and Accessibility

Optimize text display and reading experience.

### Accessing Font Settings

1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Click "Font & Accessibility" tab
3. Configure text options
4. Preview in real-time

### Font Selection

**Available Fonts:**

1. **Default Font**
   - System font (varies by device)
   - Optimized for readability
   - Good performance
   - Most familiar appearance

2. **OpenDyslexic**
   - Designed for dyslexia readers
   - Distinct letter shapes (prevents reversals)
   - Heavier baseline
   - Supports better text flow

3. **Atkinson Hyperlegible**
   - Designed for extreme readability
   - High contrast characters
   - Large X-height
   - Helps with vision impairment
   - WCAG AAA compliant

**How to Change Font:**
1. Go to Font Settings
2. Click desired font option
3. Preview text updates
4. Click "Save"
5. Font applies site-wide

**Font Details:**

| Font | Best For | Characteristics |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| Default | General use | System optimized |
| OpenDyslexic | Dyslexia | Distinctive shapes |
| Atkinson | Low vision | Maximum contrast |

### Text Scale

**Adjust Text Size:**
- Slider from 80% to 150%
- 100% is default size
- Live preview shows changes
- Affects all text on platform

**Presets:**
- Smaller (85%)
- Default (100%)
- Larger (115%)
- Extra Large (130%)
- Huge (150%)

**How to Use:**
1. Go to Font Settings > Text Scale
2. Drag slider or click preset
3. Preview text updates in real-time
4. Find comfortable size
5. Click "Save"

**Text Scale Tips:**
- 100% is standard size
- Each increase is ~5% larger
- Decreases scroll efficiency (more to read)
- 150% is maximum for readability

## Account Deletion

Permanently remove your Milonexa account and all associated data.

### Before Deleting Your Account

**What You Should Do:**
1. Export your data first (see Data Export section)
2. Download backup of posts/photos
3. Note important information
4. Disconnect OAuth accounts if desired
5. Save recovery codes (if applicable)

**What Will Happen:**
- Account deleted permanently
- All data removed from servers
- Cannot be recovered
- Username becomes available
- Posted content may be cached elsewhere
- No refunds for purchases

### Initiating Account Deletion

**Access Deletion Page:**
1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Scroll to bottom: "Account Management"
3. Look for "Delete Account" or similar
4. Click "Delete Account Permanently"
5. Warning page appears

**Warning Message:**
- "This action cannot be undone"
- "All your data will be permanently deleted"
- "This includes posts, messages, photos"
- "Your username will become available"
- Understand consequences before proceeding

### Account Deletion Process

**Step 1: Password Confirmation**
1. Enter your account password
2. Verify you have account access
3. If 2FA enabled, provide TOTP code
4. Click "Continue"

**Step 2: Email Confirmation**
1. Email sent to account email address
2. Subject: "Confirm Account Deletion"
3. Click link in email (valid 24 hours)
4. Taken to final confirmation page

**Step 3: Final Confirmation**
1. Read: "You are about to permanently delete your account"
2. Understand you cannot undo
3. Confirm by entering email address
4. Click "Delete My Account"
5. Account deleted

**Step 4: Completion**
- Confirmation message: "Your account has been deleted"
- Session logged out
- Redirect to goodbye page
- Data deleted within 24 hours

### Post-Deletion

**Timeline:**
- Account deleted immediately
- Posts removed from feed within 1 hour
- Profile no longer searchable
- Messages may remain in recipients' inboxes
- Username available for reuse after 30 days

**Recovery Option:**
- Within 24 hours: Contact support to restore
- After 24 hours: Permanent, non-recoverable
- No exceptions to permanent deletion
- Data cannot be recovered

**Account Reactivation:**
- Cannot reactivate deleted account
- Can create new account with same email (after 30 days)
- New account is completely separate
- No connection to deleted account

### If You Delete by Mistake

**Recovery Process (Within 24 Hours):**
1. Contact support@milonexa.com immediately
2. Subject: "Account Deletion Recovery"
3. Provide username and email
4. Explain accidental deletion
5. Support may restore within 24 hours

**After 24 Hours:**
- Account cannot be recovered
- Data permanently deleted
- Create new account if desired
- Must wait 30 days to use same email
