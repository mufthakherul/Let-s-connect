# Operations Runbook

This page summarizes incident response and operational recovery practices.

## Severity

- Sev-1: major outage / critical data risk
- Sev-2: major feature degradation
- Sev-3: moderate degradation
- Sev-4: minor defect

## Incident workflow

1. Acknowledge and classify severity.
2. Assign incident commander.
3. Contain impact and stabilize.
4. Diagnose root cause.
5. Restore service and verify.
6. Publish PIR with action items.
