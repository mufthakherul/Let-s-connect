## 1. Creating an Account

### Requirements

| Field | Rules |
|-------|-------|
| **Username** | 3–30 characters · letters, numbers, underscores only · must be unique |
| **Email** | Valid address · one per account |
| **Password** | 8+ characters · must include uppercase, lowercase, number, special character |
| **Display Name** | Any name you choose · can include spaces |
| **Date of Birth** | Required · minimum age 13 |

### Steps

1. Click **Sign Up** on the Milonexa homepage.
2. Complete the registration form.
3. Accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**.
4. Click **Create Account**.
5. Check your email for a verification code.

> 🔒 Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

## 2. Email Verification

After registration, we send a **6-digit code** to your email address.

1. Open the verification email (subject: *"Verify your Milonexa account"*).
2. Enter the code on the verification screen.
3. Your account is activated immediately.

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your **spam / junk folder**.
- Wait up to 2 minutes before requesting a resend.
- Click **Resend Code** on the verification page.
- Make sure you typed your email address correctly during registration.

> ⚠️ The verification code expires after **15 minutes**. Request a new one if it expires.

## 3. Logging In

### Standard Login

1. Click **Log In** on the homepage.
2. Enter your **email address** or **username**.
3. Enter your **password**.
4. Click **Sign In**.

### Staying Signed In

- Check **"Remember me"** to stay logged in on trusted devices.
- For security, sessions automatically expire after 30 days of inactivity.
- You can view and manage all active sessions under **Settings → Security → Active Sessions**.

## 4. Social Login (OAuth)

Sign in instantly with an existing account — no password needed.

| Provider | Availability |
|----------|-------------|
| 🔵 **Google** | ✅ Available |
| ⚫ **GitHub** | ✅ Available |
| 💜 **Discord** | ✅ Available |
| 🍎 **Apple** | ✅ Available |

**How to use OAuth:**
1. Click **Log In** (or **Sign Up**) on the homepage.
2. Click one of the social login provider buttons.
3. Authorise Milonexa on the provider's page.
4. You're logged in — a Milonexa account is created automatically if it's your first time.

**Linking additional providers:**  
Go to **Settings → Security → Connected Accounts** to link or unlink providers.

## 5. Password Reset

Forgot your password? No problem.

1. Click **Log In** → **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the **email address** associated with your account.
3. Click **Send Reset Link**.
4. Check your email for a reset link (expires in **1 hour**).
5. Click the link and enter a new password.
6. Click **Save new password** — you're logged in automatically.

> 💡 If you signed up using a social provider (Google, Discord, etc.) and never set a password, use the same provider to log in or set a password under **Settings → Security**.

## 6. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of security beyond your password.

### Enabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Enable 2FA**.
3. Choose your method:
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended) — works with Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, etc.
   - **Email Code** — receive a code by email each time you log in
4. For authenticator apps: scan the QR code with your app.
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to confirm.
6. Save your **backup codes** in a secure place.

### Logging In with 2FA

1. Enter your email/username and password.
2. You're prompted for your 6-digit authenticator code.
3. Open your authenticator app and enter the current code.
4. Click **Verify**.

### Backup Codes

- Generated when you enable 2FA.
- Each code can only be used once.
- Store them securely (e.g., a password manager or printed and locked away).
- Regenerate codes at **Settings → Security → 2FA → Regenerate backup codes**.

> ⚠️ If you lose access to your authenticator and have no backup codes, account recovery requires identity verification via support.

## 7. Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile is your public page on Milonexa — make it represent you well.

### Profile Picture

- Recommended size: **400 × 400 pixels**
- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF (max 5 MB)
- Displayed as a circle throughout the platform

### Cover Photo

- Recommended size: **1200 × 400 pixels** (3:1 ratio)
- Appears at the top of your profile page
- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (max 10 MB)

### Bio

- Maximum **250 characters**
- Appears beneath your name on your profile
- Supports text only (no markdown in the bio field)
- Tip: include your interests, profession, or what you post about

### Additional Profile Fields

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Location** | City, country, or region — optional and privately controlled |
| **Website** | A link to your site, portfolio, or social media |
| **Birthday** | Shown as day/month (year hidden by default) |
| **Pronouns** | Displayed next to your name |
| **Skills** | Tags for your expertise — others can endorse you |

### Editing Your Profile

1. Click your **avatar** in the navigation → **Edit Profile**.
2. Make changes and click **Save Changes**.

## 8. Profile Visibility

Control who can see your profile and content.

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Profile visibility** | Everyone (Public) · Friends only · Private |
| **Post default visibility** | Public · Friends · Private (can change per post) |
| **Friend list visibility** | Everyone · Friends · Only me |
| **Email address visibility** | Only me |
| **Date of birth visibility** | Public / Month and day only / Only me |
| **Activity status** | Show when active / Hidden |

Configure these at **Settings → Privacy**.

## 9. Account Settings

Access all settings at **Settings** in the navigation menu.

| Category | What's Inside |
|----------|--------------|
| **Profile** | Edit bio, photo, cover image, links |
| **Privacy** | Post visibility, search visibility, who can follow/friend you |
| **Security** | Password, 2FA, connected accounts, active sessions |
| **Notifications** | Choose what alerts you receive and how |
| **Appearance** | Dark mode, light mode, font size, colour theme |
| **Language & Region** | Interface language, date/time format, timezone |
| **Email Preferences** | Marketing emails, weekly digest, notification emails |
| **Data & Privacy** | Download your data, manage connected apps |
| **Accessibility** | Reduce motion, high contrast, screen reader options |

## 10. Changing Username or Email

### Change Username

1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Username**.
2. Type your new username.
3. Click **Save**.

> ⚠️ You can only change your username **once every 30 days**.

### Change Email Address

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Email Address**.
2. Enter your new email address.
3. Enter your current password to confirm.
4. A verification email is sent to the **new** address.
5. Click the link in the verification email.
6. Your email is updated.

## 11. Closing Your Account

### Deactivate (Temporary)

Deactivating hides your profile and content without deleting anything.

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Deactivate Account**.
2. Confirm your reason (optional).
3. Enter your password and click **Deactivate**.
4. Log back in any time to reactivate.

### Delete (Permanent)

> ⚠️ **Account deletion is permanent.** All your posts, messages, and data are removed and cannot be recovered.

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**.
2. Read the confirmation notice carefully.
3. Enter your password and click **Delete my account permanently**.
4. You'll receive a confirmation email and have **14 days** to cancel by logging back in.

**What gets deleted:**
- Your profile and all profile data
- All posts, comments, and reactions you made
- Your message history
- Your groups membership and created groups (if you're the only admin)
- Your orders history (kept for legal compliance, per privacy policy)
