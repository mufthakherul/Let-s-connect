## 1. What You Can Bookmark

You can bookmark virtually any content on the platform:

| Content Type | Where to Find the Bookmark Button |
|-------------|----------------------------------|
| **Posts** | Under any post (🔖 icon) |
| **Blog articles** | Inside the article |
| **Videos** | Below the video player |
| **Products (Marketplace)** | On product pages (🔖 or ♡ Save) |
| **Groups** | On the group page (Save Group) |
| **User profiles** | On profiles (🔖 Save) |
| **Pages** | On the Page (Save Page) |
| **Events** | On event listings |
| **Collaboration documents** | In the document header |

## 2. Saving a Bookmark

### Quick Bookmark

1. Find the 🔖 (bookmark) icon on a piece of content.
2. Click it.
3. The icon fills in — it's saved to your **default bookmark collection**.

### Save to a Specific Collection

1. Click the 🔖 icon but **hold** (or long-press on mobile) to get options, OR
2. Click 🔖 → **Save to Collection** from the dropdown.
3. Choose an existing collection or create a new one.
4. Click **Save**.

## 3. Viewing Your Bookmarks

Access all bookmarks from:
- **Profile → Bookmarks** (in the navigation menu)
- Or **/bookmarks** in the URL

### Bookmark Page Layout

Your bookmarks page shows:
- All saved items sorted by most recently saved
- Tabs for each collection
- Search bar to find a specific bookmarked item
- Filter by content type (Posts, Videos, Products, etc.)

## 4. Organising with Collections

Collections are like folders for your bookmarks — group related saves together.

### Create a Collection

1. Go to **Bookmarks → + New Collection**.
2. Enter a name (e.g., "Articles to Read", "Recipe Ideas", "Gift Ideas").
3. Choose a cover image (optional).
4. Set visibility: **Private** (only you) or **Shared** (invite collaborators).
5. Click **Create**.

### Move Bookmarks between Collections

1. In your bookmarks, find the item.
2. Click ⋮ → **Move to Collection**.
3. Select the destination collection.

### Rename or Delete a Collection

1. Go to **Bookmarks → Collections**.
2. Click ⋮ on the collection.
3. Select **Rename** or **Delete**.

> ⚠️ Deleting a collection removes all bookmarks within it.

---

## 5. Removing Bookmarks

### Remove a Single Bookmark

- Click the 🔖 icon on a bookmarked item (it toggles off), or
- In your bookmarks page: ⋮ → **Remove**

### Remove All from a Collection

1. Open the collection.
2. Click **⋮ → Remove all items**.
3. Confirm.

---

## 6. Sharing a Collection

Share a curated collection with others.

1. Open the collection.
2. Click **Share Collection**.
3. Choose:
   - **Share link** — anyone with the link can view it
   - **Invite collaborators** — specific users can add bookmarks to the shared collection
4. Copy and share the link.

> Collections are private by default. Only collections you explicitly share are visible to others.
