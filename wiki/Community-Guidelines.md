## Our Values

Milonexa is built on three core values:

- 🤝 **Respect** — Treat every person the way you would want to be treated
- 🌐 **Inclusion** — Everyone is welcome regardless of background, identity, or belief
- 🔒 **Safety** — The platform should be a safe space for genuine expression

These guidelines apply to **all content and behaviour** on the platform, including posts, messages, comments, groups, profiles, and live streams.

---

## Be Respectful

We encourage lively debate and different perspectives, but all interaction must remain respectful.

### ✅ Do

- Engage constructively with opinions you disagree with
- Acknowledge that people have different backgrounds and experiences
- Use welcoming, inclusive language
- Give credit when sharing or building on others' ideas
- Report content that makes you feel unsafe (use the report button)

### ❌ Don't

- Use slurs, hate speech, or dehumanising language
- Bully, harass, threaten, or intimidate other users
- Deliberately provoke or "troll" people
- Post personal information about someone to embarrass or harm them
- Send unsolicited graphic content

---

## Prohibited Content

The following content is **strictly prohibited** and will be removed:

### Hate Speech
Content that attacks people based on:
- Race or ethnicity
- National origin or citizenship
- Religion or religious beliefs
- Gender or gender identity
- Sexual orientation
- Disability or health status
- Age

### Violence & Graphic Content
- Threats of violence against individuals or groups
- Content celebrating or glorifying violence
- Graphic images of injury, death, or gore without clear public-interest context
- Content promoting or instructing harmful acts

### Adult & Explicit Content
- Sexually explicit content is not permitted in public areas of the platform
- Explicit content is strictly prohibited involving minors under any circumstances
- Nudity in profile pictures or cover photos is not permitted

### Illegal Content
- Content that violates applicable laws
- Content promoting or facilitating illegal activities (drug dealing, theft, fraud, etc.)
- Counterfeit goods and services
- Content that violates third-party intellectual property rights

### Child Safety
- Any content that sexualises, endangers, or exploits minors
- Grooming behaviour or attempts to solicit minors
- Milonexa has zero tolerance for violations in this category — all such content is reported to relevant authorities

---

## Prohibited Behaviour

### Harassment & Bullying
- Persistent unwanted contact after someone asks you to stop
- Targeting someone with negative content intended to harm them
- Coordinating with others to harass a specific person ("pile-ons")
- Impersonating someone to damage their reputation

### Doxxing
Publishing private information about a person without their consent, including:
- Home address or location
- Phone number
- Workplace
- Financial details
- Personal relationships

### Threats & Incitement
- Direct or indirect threats of physical harm
- Inciting others to commit violence or illegal acts
- Encouraging self-harm or suicide

### Spam
- Mass-sending identical messages to multiple users
- Posting repetitive, low-quality content to increase visibility
- Buying, selling, or trading followers, likes, or engagement
- Using bots or automated tools to inflate metrics artificially

## Content & Copyright

### Your Original Content
When you post original content, you retain ownership. By posting on Milonexa, you grant us a licence to display it on the platform (as described in our Terms of Service).

### Third-Party Content
- Only share content you own or have permission to share
- Credit original creators when sharing their work
- Do not post content that violates someone else's copyright

### DMCA & Takedowns
If you believe content on Milonexa infringes your copyright:
1. Submit a DMCA takedown request via the report system or by contacting support.
2. We will review and act within the timeframes required by applicable law.
3. Repeated copyright violations may result in account termination.

---

## Privacy of Others

You must respect the privacy of other users and third parties.

- Do not share someone's personal information without their consent
- Do not record, screenshot, or distribute private conversations without the consent of all parties
- Do not post images or videos of others without their consent, particularly in private settings
- Do not attempt to identify or unmask anonymous or pseudonymous users

## Safety & Wellbeing

### Self-Harm & Suicide
Content that promotes, glorifies, or provides detailed methods for self-harm or suicide is not permitted. If you or someone you know is struggling:

- **International Association for Suicide Prevention:** https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
- **Crisis Text Line (US):** Text HOME to 741741
- **Samaritans (UK/Ireland):** 116 123

Milonexa may proactively share mental health resources when certain topics are searched.

### Dangerous Challenges & Activities
Content encouraging dangerous physical challenges (especially to younger audiences) that could result in serious injury is prohibited.

### Eating Disorders & Body Image
Content promoting harmful eating behaviours or extreme body modification in a glorifying manner is not permitted.

## Misinformation

Milonexa takes misinformation seriously, particularly regarding:

- Public health and safety emergencies
- Electoral processes and voting
- Scientific consensus on topics like climate change and vaccine safety
- Impersonation of authoritative sources (news organisations, governments)

**How we handle it:**
- Confirmed false information may be labelled with a fact-check notice
- In severe cases, particularly harmful misinformation may be removed
- Repeated deliberate spreading of misinformation may lead to account action

> We aim to support free expression and debate while preventing harm. Disputed topics are handled carefully to avoid over-removal of legitimate discussion.

## Spam & Fake Accounts

### Spam
Do not use Milonexa to distribute:
- Unsolicited commercial messages
- Phishing links or malware
- Fake engagement (buying likes, followers, or engagement)

### Fake Accounts & Impersonation
- You may not impersonate another real person or public figure
- Parody and satire accounts must be clearly labelled as such
- Do not create multiple accounts to evade bans or restrictions
- Automated or bot accounts must be clearly identified and must not violate these guidelines

## Reporting Violations

Anyone can report content or behaviour they believe violates these guidelines.

### How to Report

**Report a Post:**
1. Click the **⋮ (More options)** menu on the post.
2. Select **Report**.
3. Choose the reason.
4. Add any context (optional).
5. Submit.

**Report a User:**
1. Go to the user's profile.
2. Click **⋮** → **Report @username**.
3. Choose the reason and submit.

**Report a Message:**
1. In the conversation, hover over the message.
2. Click the **Flag** icon → **Report**.

**Report a Group:**
1. In the group, click **⋮** → **Report Group**.

All reports are **confidential**. We do not notify the reported user who submitted the report.

## Enforcement & Consequences

When a violation is confirmed, Milonexa may take the following actions depending on severity:

| Action | When Applied |
|--------|-------------|
| **Content removal** | Content that violates guidelines is removed |
| **Warning** | First or minor violations |
| **Temporary suspension** | Repeated or moderate violations |
| **Feature restriction** | Loss of posting, messaging, or commenting privileges |
| **Permanent ban** | Severe or repeated violations, particularly involving safety |
| **Law enforcement referral** | Illegal content, threats, or child safety violations |

We aim to take **proportional action** and consider context when making enforcement decisions.

---

## Appeals

If you believe an action taken against your account or content was a mistake, you can appeal.

### How to Appeal

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Submit an Appeal**.
2. Describe why you believe the action was incorrect.
3. Provide any supporting context or evidence.
4. Submit your appeal.

Our Trust & Safety team reviews all appeals. We aim to respond within **5–10 business days**.

> Appeals are not available for certain severe violations (e.g., child safety violations, repeat serious offenders).
