## 1. Types of Content You Can Create

| Content Type | Where | Best For |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| **Text post** | Feed, Groups, Pages | Quick updates, thoughts, announcements |
| **Photo post** | Feed, Groups, Pages | Visual sharing |
| **Video post** | Feed, Groups, Pages | Short-form video |
| **Blog/Article** | Profile, Pages | Long-form writing, tutorials, opinion pieces |

## 2. Writing Posts

### Compose Box

The compose box is available at the top of:
- Your **home feed**
- Your **profile**
- Any **group** you're a member of
- Any **Page** you manage

### Post Length

- Regular posts: up to **5,000 characters**
- Blog articles: no character limit
- Comments: up to 2,000 characters

### Text Formatting

Use markdown-style shortcuts or toolbar buttons:

| Format | Shortcut | Example |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **Bold** | `**text**` or Ctrl+B | **This is bold** |
| *Italic* | `*text*` or Ctrl+I | *This is italic* |
| ~~Strikethrough~~ | `~~text~~` | ~~Struck through~~ |
| `Code` | `` `code` `` | `inline code` |
| [Link](url) | `[text](url)` or Ctrl+K | [Click here](https://milonexa.com) |
| Heading | `# Heading` | Large heading text |
| Bullet list | `- item` | • Bullet item |
| Numbered list | `1. item` | 1. First item |

### Hashtags & Mentions

- Add **#hashtags** to make your post discoverable
- @mention users and pages using `@username`
- Mentioned users receive a notification

### Emoji

- Click the 😊 icon in the compose toolbar
- Or type `:emoji_name:` (autocomplete appears)

## 3. Photo & Video Posts

### Photos

- **Maximum:** 4 images per post
- **Formats:** JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF (animated GIFs supported)
- **Max size:** 10 MB per image
- **Recommendation:** Use high-quality images at 1080px wide minimum

**Add alt text** (accessibility): After uploading, click the image → **Edit** → add a description.

### Videos

- **Maximum:** 1 video per post
- **Formats:** MP4, WebM, MOV (MP4/H.264 recommended)
- **Max duration:** 10 minutes
- **Max file size:** 100 MB
- Videos are **auto-transcoded** to multiple quality levels (360p, 720p, 1080p)
- A thumbnail is auto-generated; you can upload a custom thumbnail

### Photo Albums

Create a standalone album in your media gallery:
1. Go to **Profile → Photos → + Create Album**.
2. Name the album.
3. Upload photos.
4. Set visibility (Public, Friends, Private).

## 4. Long-Form Blog Posts

Write articles with the full-featured blog editor.

### Opening the Blog Editor

1. Click **✍️ Write an article** (on your profile or feed) or
2. Go to **Profile → Blog → + New Post**

### Blog Editor Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|------------|
| **Rich text formatting** | Full heading levels, bold, italic, tables, lists |
| **Cover image** | Full-width hero image at the top of the article |
| **Inline images** | Embed images throughout the text |
| **Code blocks** | Syntax-highlighted code with language selector |
| **Embed media** | Embed YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter, and more |
| **Reading time estimate** | Automatic calculation shown to readers |
| **Table of contents** | Auto-generated from your headings |
| **Tags** | Add up to 10 tags for discoverability |
| **Series** | Group related articles into a series |
| **SEO settings** | Custom meta title and description |

### Publishing vs. Saving as Draft

- Click **Publish** to make the article live
- Click **Save Draft** to save without publishing
- Click **Preview** to see how it looks to readers

### Featured Articles

Your best articles can be **pinned** to the top of your profile:
1. On the article → ⋮ → **Feature on Profile**.
2. Only 1 article can be featured at a time.

## 5. Polls

Polls let you ask your audience a question and collect votes.

### Creating a Poll

1. In the compose box, click the **☑️ Poll** icon.
2. Write your question.
3. Add options (2–6 choices).
4. Set the **poll duration**: 1 day / 3 days / 7 days / custom.
5. Optionally allow multiple choice.
6. Post the poll.

### Poll Results

- Results are visible to everyone after they vote (or when the poll ends)
- You can see who voted for what option (unless you make the poll anonymous)
- Anonymous polls: toggle **Anonymous voting** in the poll settings

### Ending a Poll Early

Go to your post → ⋮ → **End Poll**. Results are finalised immediately.

## 6. Pages & Creator Tools

Pages are public-facing profiles for creators, brands, and organisations.

### Creating a Page

1. Go to **Pages → + Create Page**.
2. Choose page type:
   - **Creator** — for individuals with an audience
   - **Business** — for companies and organisations
   - **Community** — for fan pages, clubs, interest groups
3. Fill in page details: name, description, category, website.
4. Upload page picture and cover.
5. Click **Create Page**.

### Page vs. Personal Profile

| Feature | Personal Profile | Page |
|---------|----------------|------|
| Audience relationship | Friends (mutual) | Followers (one-way) |
| Privacy options | Full range | Mostly public |
| Analytics | Basic | Advanced (likes, reach, demographics) |
| Advertising | No | Yes |
| Multiple admins | No | Yes |
| Verification eligibility | No | Yes |

### Managing Multiple Pages

You can manage all your pages from **Pages → Your Pages**. Switch between them using the page switcher in the top navigation.

## 7. Content Scheduling

Plan and queue posts to publish at a specific time.

### Schedule a Post

1. Write your post as normal.
2. Click the **⏰ Schedule** button (next to Publish).
3. Choose date and time (your local timezone is used by default).
4. Click **Schedule**.

### Manage Scheduled Posts

**Profile → Scheduled** (or **Page → Scheduled**) shows all upcoming posts. You can:
- Edit the content
- Change the scheduled time
- Cancel the schedule (publishes immediately or saves as draft)

## 8. Content Analytics

Understand how your content is performing.

### Post-Level Analytics

Click **⋮ → View Analytics** on any post to see:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|------------|
| **Impressions** | How many times the post was displayed |
| **Reach** | How many unique accounts saw it |
| **Engagements** | Total interactions (reactions, comments, shares, clicks) |
| **Link clicks** | Clicks on any links in the post |
| **Video views** | Views and average watch time (for video posts) |

### Profile & Page Analytics

Go to **Profile → Analytics** (or **Page → Insights**) for aggregate data:
- Follower growth over time
- Top-performing posts
- Audience demographics (for Pages)
- Best times to post

## 9. Drafts

Posts and articles saved as drafts are not published and are only visible to you.

### Finding Drafts

- **Feed posts:** Profile → ⋮ → Drafts
- **Blog articles:** Profile → Blog → Drafts tab

### Auto-Save

The editor auto-saves your draft every 30 seconds while you type.

## 10. Editing & Deleting Content

### Edit a Post

1. Click **⋮** on your post.
2. Select **Edit**.
3. Make changes.
4. Click **Save**.

> Posts display an *"(edited)"* label after being changed.

### Delete a Post

1. Click **⋮** on your post.
2. Select **Delete**.
3. Confirm deletion.

> ⚠️ Deleted posts cannot be recovered. If you might want the post later, edit instead of delete.

### Archive a Post

Instead of deleting, you can **archive** a post to hide it from your profile without losing it:
1. ⋮ → **Archive Post**
2. Find it later in **Profile → Archived Posts**
3. Click **Unarchive** to restore

## 11. Copyright & Responsible Posting

### Before You Post

- Only share content **you own** or have permission to share
- Credit the original creator when sharing their work
- Do not use copyrighted music in videos without a licence
- Do not post content that reveals someone's private information
- Follow the [Community Guidelines](Community-Guidelines) for all content

### AI-Assisted Content

Content created with AI assistance may be posted, but:
- Clearly label AI-generated images or articles
- Do not use AI to impersonate real people
- Do not spread AI-generated misinformation

### Reporting Copyright Violations

If you see your content being used without permission:
1. Click ⋮ on the post → **Report** → **Copyright Infringement**
2. Fill in the details of your claim
3. Submit

We review and act on valid copyright reports as required by applicable law.
