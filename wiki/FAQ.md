## Account & Login

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
Visit the Milonexa homepage and click **Sign Up**. Fill in your username, email, password, and date of birth. You'll need to verify your email to activate your account. See [Getting Started](Getting-Started).

---

**Q: I didn't receive my verification email. What should I do?**  
Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, wait 2 minutes and click **Resend Code** on the verification page. Make sure you typed your email address correctly.

---

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
Click **Log In → Forgot password?**, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox. The link expires in 1 hour. See [Account & Profile → Password Reset](Account-and-Profile#5-password-reset).

---

**Q: Can I log in with Google, GitHub, or Discord?**  
Yes. On the login page, click the Google, GitHub, Discord, or Apple button to sign in without a password. See [Account & Profile → Social Login](Account-and-Profile#4-social-login-oauth).

---

**Q: My account was deactivated. How do I reactivate it?**  
Simply log in again — deactivated accounts reactivate automatically on login. If your account was suspended, contact support.

---

**Q: Can I have more than one account?**  
Each person may have one personal account. You can also create up to 5 Pages for brands, projects, or interests.

---

**Q: How do I delete my account permanently?**  
Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Deletion is permanent — all data is removed. You have 14 days after confirming to cancel by logging back in. See [Account & Profile → Closing Your Account](Account-and-Profile#11-closing-your-account).

## Profile & Privacy

**Q: How do I change my profile picture?**  
Click your avatar in the navigation → **Edit Profile** → upload a new picture.

---

**Q: Can I change my username?**  
Yes, once every 30 days. Go to **Settings → Profile → Username**, update it, and save.

---

**Q: How do I make my profile private?**  
Go to **Settings → Privacy → Profile Visibility → Friends Only** (or **Private**). See [Privacy & Security](Privacy-and-Security).

---

**Q: Can other users see my email address?**  
No. Your email address is always private and is never shown to other users.

---

**Q: How do I block someone?**  
Visit their profile → click **⋮ (More options)** → **Block @username**. They won't be able to see your profile or message you. See [Privacy & Security → Blocking](Privacy-and-Security#3-blocking-and-restricting-users).

---

**Q: What happens when I block someone?**  
They cannot see your profile, posts, or stories. They cannot message you. They are not notified they've been blocked.

---

## Social Features

**Q: What's the difference between following and friends?**  
**Following** is one-directional (like Twitter) — you see their posts but they don't need to follow you back. **Friends** is mutual — both parties accept, similar to Facebook. You can do both on Milonexa.

---

**Q: Why can't I see someone's posts?**  
Either their account is private (friends only or private), you're blocked by them, or they've restricted your interactions.

---

**Q: How do I control who sees my posts?**  
When composing a post, choose **Public**, **Friends**, or **Private** from the audience selector. You can also set a default in **Settings → Privacy → Default Post Visibility**.

---

**Q: What are hashtags used for?**  
Hashtags make your posts discoverable by topic. Anyone searching `#travel` will see posts using that tag. You can also follow hashtags to see related content in your feed.

---

**Q: How does the feed algorithm work?**  
Your feed is ranked using recency, your engagement history, your relationship strength with the poster, and overall post engagement. See [Social Features → Feed Algorithm](Social-Features#12-feed-algorithm).

---

## Messaging

**Q: Can I message anyone on the platform?**  
By default, yes — but users can restrict who can message them (everyone / friends only / nobody). If someone has restricted messages to friends only and you're not friends, you can't initiate a conversation.

---

**Q: Are my messages private?**  
Direct messages are end-to-end encrypted. Group conversations and server messages are encrypted in transit.

---

**Q: Can I send voice messages?**  
Yes. In the message input, tap/click the 🎤 microphone icon to record and send a voice note.

---

**Q: What is a Community Server?**  
It's a Discord-style community space with text channels and voice rooms for groups. Useful for larger communities that need organised discussion. See [Messaging → Community Servers](Messaging#5-community-servers).

---

**Q: How many people can be in a group conversation?**  
Up to **250 participants** in a group conversation. For larger communities, use a Community Server instead.

## Videos & Streaming

**Q: How do I upload a video?**  
Go to **Videos → + Upload** (or use the compose box). Maximum file size is 100 MB (free accounts) or 1 GB (premium). See [Videos & Media → Uploading](Videos-and-Media#3-uploading-videos).

---

**Q: Why is my uploaded video still processing?**  
Videos are transcoded after upload (converted to multiple quality levels). This typically takes a few minutes. You'll receive a notification when it's ready.

---

**Q: Is the Live TV really free?**  
Yes. The IPTV streaming directory with 60,000+ channels is free for all accounts. See [Streaming](Streaming).

---

**Q: A channel won't play. What's wrong?**  
Channel availability depends on the stream being live and not geo-restricted. Try refreshing or a similar channel. Some channels may be offline temporarily.

---

**Q: Can I watch TV channels offline?**  
No — live streams require an active internet connection.

## Marketplace & Shop

**Q: How do I start selling?**  
Go to **Shop → Sell → Set Up Your Shop**. You'll need to verify your account and add payout details (via Stripe). See [Shop & Marketplace → Selling](Shop#7-selling-on-milonexa).

---

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, and Google Pay via Stripe. Methods may vary by region.

---

**Q: Is it safe to buy on Milonexa?**  
Yes. All payments go through Stripe (a globally trusted payment provider). Your card details are never stored by Milonexa. See [Shop → Marketplace Safety](Shop#10-marketplace-safety).

---

**Q: How do I request a refund?**  
Go to **Orders → [Order] → Request Return/Refund**. The seller has 3 business days to respond. If unresolved, open a dispute. See [Shop → Returns & Refunds](Shop#6-returns--refunds).

---

**Q: Can I sell digital products?**  
Yes. When creating a listing, select "Digital Product" and upload the file. Buyers receive a download link after payment.

## Collaboration Tools

**Q: Can multiple people edit a document at the same time?**  
Yes. Milonexa documents support real-time collaborative editing with visible cursors and live updates. See [Collaboration Tools → Documents](Collaboration#2-documents).

---

**Q: How is a Wiki different from a Document?**  
A document is a single page; a Wiki is a collection of linked, versioned pages — ideal for a knowledge base, manual, or FAQ. Both support collaboration.

---

**Q: Can I export my documents?**  
Yes. Documents can be exported as PDF, DOCX, Markdown, or HTML. Click **⋮ → Export** in any document.

---

## Notifications

**Q: How do I turn off notifications for a specific post?**  
Click **⋮** on the post → **Turn off notifications for this post**.

---

**Q: Why am I getting too many email notifications?**  
Go to **Settings → Notifications → Email** and turn off the categories you don't want. See [Notifications → Email Notifications](Notifications#5-email-notifications).

---

**Q: Can I pause all notifications for a while?**  
Yes — use **Do Not Disturb** mode. **Settings → Notifications → Do Not Disturb** → set a time range. See [Notifications → Do Not Disturb](Notifications#7-muting-notifications).

## Technical & Performance

**Q: Milonexa is loading slowly. What can I do?**  
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection speed
- Disable browser extensions (some conflict with the platform)
- Install the PWA for better performance

---

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions). For the best experience, use Chrome or Edge.

---

**Q: Can I use Milonexa on my phone?**  
Yes — the platform is fully responsive. For the best mobile experience, install it as a PWA. See [Mobile & PWA](Mobile-and-PWA).

---

**Q: My camera/microphone doesn't work in meetings. Why?**  
Check that you've granted permission to Milonexa:
- **Chrome:** Address bar padlock 🔒 → Camera + Microphone → Allow
- **Safari:** Preferences → Websites → Camera / Microphone → Allow
Try refreshing after granting permission.

## Billing & Premium

**Q: Is Milonexa free?**  
Core features are free. Premium tiers with expanded storage, advanced analytics, and priority support are available. See [Platform Overview → Free vs. Premium](Platform-Overview#free-vs-premium).

---

**Q: How do I upgrade to premium?**  
**Settings → Account → Upgrade to Premium** — choose a plan and follow the payment steps.

---

**Q: How do I cancel my premium subscription?**  
**Settings → Account → Manage Subscription → Cancel**. You keep premium benefits until the end of the current billing period.

---

**Q: Can I get a refund on a premium subscription?**  
Refund eligibility depends on our billing policy and your jurisdiction's consumer protection laws. Contact support with your order details.
