## 1. Create Your Account

1. Open **Milonexa** in your browser.
2. Click **Sign Up** (top-right corner of the landing page).
3. Fill in the registration form:

| Field | Requirements |
|-------|-------------|
| **Username** | 3–30 characters, letters/numbers/underscores only |
| **Email** | A valid address you can access |
| **Password** | 8+ chars, uppercase + number + special char |
| **Display Name** | Any name (can include spaces) |
| **Date of Birth** | Required; minimum age is 13 |

4. Accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**.
5. Click **Create Account**.

> 💡 **Tip:** Choose a username you're happy with — you can only change it once every 30 days.

---

## 2. Verify Your Email

After registration, Milonexa sends a **6-digit verification code** to your email.

1. Open the email with subject **"Verify your Milonexa account"**.
2. Enter the 6-digit code on the verification screen.
3. Your account is now active.

> 📬 **No email?** Check your spam/junk folder. You can request a new code from the verification page.

---

## 3. Complete Your Profile

A complete profile helps others find and connect with you.

1. Go to **Settings → Profile** (or click your avatar).
2. Add a **profile picture** — a clear photo or avatar works best.
3. Upload a **cover photo** (recommended: 1200 × 400 px).
4. Write a short **bio** (max 250 characters) — introduce yourself!
5. Add your **location**, **website**, and any links you want to share.
6. Set your **privacy preferences** — who can see your posts and profile.
7. Click **Save Changes**.

---

## 4. Explore the Homepage

After logging in you'll land on your **Homepage Dashboard**, which shows:

- 📊 **Your activity summary** — posts, connections, engagement
- 🔔 **Notifications** — recent likes, comments, friend requests
- 💡 **Recommendations** — people to follow, groups to join
- 📰 **Quick feed** — the latest posts from your network

Navigate to **/feed** for your full social feed (all posts from friends + followed accounts).

---

## 5. Make Your First Post

1. From the homepage or feed, click **"What's on your mind?"** or the **✏️ compose button**.
2. Type your post (up to 5,000 characters).
3. Optionally add:
   - 📷 **Images** (up to 4, max 10 MB each)
   - 🎥 **Video** (up to 10 min, max 100 MB)
   - 📎 **Files** (up to 3 attachments)
   - 😊 **Emoji**, **@mentions**, or **#hashtags**
4. Choose **visibility**: Public, Friends Only, or Private.
5. Click **Post**.

---

## 6. Find and Follow People

| Method | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Search** | Use the 🔍 search bar — search by name, username, or topic |
| **Suggestions** | Check "People You May Know" on your homepage |
| **Groups** | See members of groups you join |
| **Explore** | Browse the Explore tab for trending creators |

To **follow** someone: visit their profile and click **Follow**.  
To **send a friend request**: click **Add Friend**.

---

## 7. Join a Group

1. Click **Groups** in the main navigation.
2. Browse or search for groups matching your interests.
3. Click a group name to preview it.
4. Click **Join Group** (public) or **Request to Join** (private).
5. Start participating in discussions!

> You can also **create your own group** — click **+ Create Group** and follow the setup wizard.

---

## 8. Send a Message

1. Click the **💬 Messages** icon in the navigation bar.
2. Click **+ New Message**.
3. Search for the person you want to message.
4. Type and send your first message.

You can also start a conversation directly from any user's profile by clicking **Message**.
