## 1. What Are Groups?

Groups are community spaces where members can:

- **Post content** visible only to group members
- **Discuss topics** in threaded conversations
- **Share files and media**
- **Announce events**
- **Collaborate** in a focused community

Groups differ from personal pages:
- Groups are **community-owned** — multiple admins can manage
- Members actively contribute rather than just follow
- Discussion is the primary activity

## 2. Discovering Groups

### Browse Groups

1. Click **Groups** in the main navigation.
2. Browse featured and suggested groups.
3. Filter by:
   - **Category** (Technology, Sports, Art, Education, Gaming, Health, etc.)
   - **Size** (small, medium, large)
   - **Activity** (most active, newest)

### Search for Groups

1. Use the main 🔍 **Search** bar.
2. Select the **Groups** filter.
3. Type keywords related to your interest.
4. Browse results and click to preview.

### Suggested Groups

Milonexa suggests groups based on:
- Your existing groups
- Friends' group memberships
- Your stated interests
- Posts and hashtags you engage with

Find suggestions under **Groups → Suggested for You**.

## 3. Joining a Group

### Public Groups

1. Find the group via search or browse.
2. Click **Join Group**.
3. You're a member immediately — posts and discussions are visible.

### Private (Restricted) Groups

1. Find the group.
2. Click **Request to Join**.
3. Optionally answer any membership questions set by the admin.
4. An admin reviews and approves/declines your request.
5. You receive a notification when approved.

### Secret Groups

Secret groups do not appear in search results. You must be **invited** by an existing member:
1. A member sends you an invite from within the group.
2. You receive a notification with a join link.
3. Click **Accept Invitation** to join.

## 4. Creating a Group

1. Click **Groups → + Create Group** (or the + button in the groups sidebar).
2. Enter a **Group Name** (required).
3. Write a **Description** — explain what the group is about.
4. Upload a **Group Photo** (recommended: 400 × 400 px).
5. Upload a **Cover Photo** (recommended: 1200 × 400 px).
6. Choose **Group Privacy** (Public, Private, or Secret — see below).
7. Select **Category** (helps with discoverability).
8. Configure **Membership settings** (open join, approval required, invite only).
9. Click **Create Group**.

After creation, you can:
- Invite friends to the group
- Post a welcome message
- Set up group rules
- Assign additional admins/moderators

## 5. Group Types & Privacy

| Type | Who Can Find It | Who Can See Posts | Who Can Join |
|------|----------------|------------------|-------------|
| 🌍 **Public** | Anyone | Anyone (inc. non-members) | Anyone |
| 🔒 **Private** | Anyone in search | Members only | Request required |
| 👁️ **Secret** | No (invite only) | Members only | Invite only |

### Changing Group Privacy

Group privacy can be changed by the owner:
- Public → Private: **Allowed** (existing posts become members-only)
- Private → Public: **Not allowed** (to protect member privacy)
- Any → Secret: **Allowed** with owner confirmation

## 6. Posting in Groups

### Creating Group Posts

1. Inside the group, click the **"Write something..."** composer.
2. Create your post (text, images, video, files, polls).
3. All group posts are visible to group members by default.
4. Click **Post**.

### Post Approval (in some groups)

Groups with **post approval** enabled require an admin or moderator to approve posts before they appear. You'll see a "Pending approval" notice.

### Group-Only Content

Posts made inside a group are only visible to group members (for Private/Secret groups). Public group posts can be shared and are visible to everyone.

### Pinned Posts

Group admins can **pin** important posts to the top of the group feed:
- ⋮ on any post → **Pin to Group**
- Up to 5 posts can be pinned

## 7. Group Admin & Moderation

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control; transfer ownership; delete group |
| **Admin** | Manage members, posts, settings; add/remove moderators |
| **Moderator** | Approve posts, remove posts, remove members |
| **Member** | Post and comment (based on group settings) |

### Assigning Roles

1. Go to **Group → Members**.
2. Find the member.
3. Click **⋮** → **Make Admin** or **Make Moderator**.

### Managing Members

From **Group → Admin Panel → Members**:
- Search and filter members
- See join date and activity
- Remove members
- Block from group (prevents rejoining)

### Setting Group Rules

1. Go to **Group → Admin Panel → Group Rules**.
2. Click **+ Add Rule**.
3. Write the rule title and description.
4. Save.

Rules appear in the group sidebar and are shown to new members when they join.

### Post Approval

Enable post approval: **Admin Panel → Settings → Post Approval → On**.

All new posts go to a moderation queue before publishing.

## 8. Group Events & Announcements

### Creating an Announcement

1. Use the composer and select the **📢 Announcement** type.
2. Only admins and moderators can post announcements.
3. Announcements appear at the top of the group feed and send notifications to all members.

### Group Events

1. In the group, click **Events → + Create Event**.
2. Fill in event details:
   - Name and description
   - Date and time
   - Location (physical or virtual meeting link)
   - Cover image
3. Click **Publish**.

Members can RSVP: **Going**, **Maybe**, or **Can't Go**.

Event participants are notified of reminders automatically.

## 9. Leaving a Group

### Leave as a Member

1. Go to the group.
2. Click **Joined** → **Leave Group**.
3. Confirm.

Your posts remain in the group after you leave (unless you delete them first).

### Transfer Ownership Before Leaving

If you're the group owner:
1. Assign another admin as owner first: **Admin Panel → Members → [Member] → Make Owner**.
2. Then leave.

> If the owner leaves without transferring ownership, the group may be assigned to another admin, or frozen pending admin assignment.

## 10. Reporting & Safety in Groups

### Report a Post in a Group

1. Click **⋮** on the post.
2. Select **Report to Milonexa** (to report a platform violation) or **Report to Admin** (for group-specific issues).

### Report the Entire Group

1. In the group, click **⋮** (top of the group page).
2. Select **Report Group**.
3. Choose a reason and submit.

### Group Member Safety

Members can report other members directly from the group:
1. Click on the member's name.
2. Click **⋮ → Report**.
