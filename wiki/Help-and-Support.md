## 2. Reporting Content or Users

### Report a Post

1. Click **⋮ (More options)** on the post.
2. Select **Report**.
3. Choose a reason:
   - Spam
   - Misinformation
   - Harassment or bullying
   - Hate speech
   - Violence or dangerous content
   - Nudity or sexual content
   - Copyright infringement
   - Other
4. Add optional details.
5. Click **Submit Report**.

### Report a User

1. Go to the user's profile.
2. Click **⋮** → **Report @username**.
3. Choose a reason and submit.

### Report a Message

1. Hover over the message.
2. Click **⋮ → Report**.

### Report a Group

1. In the group header: **⋮ → Report Group**.

### Report a Marketplace Listing

1. On the product page: **⋮ → Report Listing**.

### What Happens After You Report?

- Reports are reviewed by our **Trust & Safety team**
- You'll receive a notification when the review is complete
- For non-urgent reports, expect a decision within **48–72 hours**
- Your identity is kept confidential — the reported user is not told who reported them

## 3. Account Issues

### Locked Out of Your Account

If you can't log in:

1. **Forgot password?** → Use the [password reset flow](Account-and-Profile#5-password-reset).
2. **2FA code not working?** → Use a backup code. See [Account & Profile → 2FA](Account-and-Profile#6-two-factor-authentication-2fa).
3. **Lost 2FA and backup codes?** → Contact support (identity verification required).
4. **Account suspended?** → Contact support to understand the reason and appeal.
5. **Account hacked?** → Reset your password immediately, then contact support.

### Account Disabled/Suspended

If your account has been disabled:
1. You'll see a notice explaining the reason.
2. Go to **Submit an Appeal** (linked from the notice) or contact support.
3. Provide your account email and any relevant context.

### Account Impersonation

If someone is impersonating you:
1. Find the fake profile.
2. **⋮ → Report → Impersonation**.
3. Provide a link to your authentic profile for verification.

## 4. Technical Support

### Before Contacting Support

**Try these steps first:**

1. **Refresh the page** — resolves many temporary issues
2. **Clear browser cache** — Chrome: Settings → More tools → Clear browsing data
3. **Try a different browser** — isolates browser-specific issues
4. **Disable browser extensions** — some extensions interfere with the platform
5. **Check your internet connection** — run a speed test at fast.com
6. **Check the [Status Page](#)** — see if there's a known platform outage

### Common Technical Issues

| Issue | Quick Fix |
|-------|-----------|
| Video won't play | Try lower quality; check connection speed (need 5+ Mbps for HD) |
| Camera/mic not working in meetings | Check browser permissions; refresh after granting |
| Notifications not appearing | Check browser notification permissions |
| PWA not installing | Use Safari on iOS; use Chrome/Edge on Android/desktop |
| Page stuck loading | Clear cache; try incognito/private window |
| Images not uploading | Check file size (max 10 MB) and format (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF) |

## 5. Privacy & Data Requests

### Request Your Data

Download a copy of all personal data Milonexa holds:
1. **Settings → Data & Privacy → Download Your Data**
2. Receive a download link by email within 24–48 hours

### Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data:
1. Delete your account: **Settings → Account → Delete Account**, or
2. Submit a data deletion request: **Settings → Data & Privacy → Submit a Data Request → Delete my data**

### GDPR / CCPA Requests

For formal data rights requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection):
1. **Settings → Data & Privacy → Submit a Data Request**
2. Select the type of request
3. Provide details

We respond to verified requests within **30 days** as required by applicable law.

If you or someone you know is in danger, contact your **local emergency services** immediately.

### Mental Health Resources

| Region | Resource | Contact |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 🌍 **International** | International Association for Suicide Prevention | https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/ |
| 🇺🇸 **United States** | 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline | Call or text **988** |
| 🇺🇸 **US** | Crisis Text Line | Text **HOME** to **741741** |
| 🇬🇧 **United Kingdom** | Samaritans | **116 123** (free, 24/7) |
| 🇦🇺 **Australia** | Beyond Blue | **1300 22 4636** |
| 🇨🇦 **Canada** | Crisis Services Canada | **1-833-456-4566** |

### Reporting Urgent Safety Issues

For content involving **imminent danger to life** (self-harm, threats to others, child abuse):
1. Report via the platform immediately (see [Reporting Content](#2-reporting-content-or-users))
2. **Emergency situations**: Contact your local emergency services directly

Our Trust & Safety team prioritises urgent safety reports and responds faster than standard reports.

## 7. Feedback & Feature Requests

### Submit Feedback

We love hearing from users! Share:
- Bug reports
- Feature ideas
- Usability feedback
- General suggestions

**In-app:** Click the **💬 Feedback** button (usually bottom-right of the page) or go to **Help → Send Feedback**.

### Feature Requests

Post and vote on feature ideas:
- **GitHub Discussions:** https://github.com/mufthakherul/Let-s-connect/discussions
- Your idea may already be submitted — search before posting

### Report a Bug

For technical bugs:
1. **Help → Report a Bug**
2. Describe:
   - What you were doing
   - What you expected to happen
   - What actually happened
   - Browser and OS version
   - Screenshot (if possible)

### Responsible Security Disclosure

Found a security vulnerability? Please report it responsibly:
1. **Do not exploit it or share it publicly first**
2. Submit via **Help → Report Security Issue** or the GitHub security advisory page
3. We'll acknowledge your report within 48 hours and credit you for responsible disclosure

## 8. Contact Support

### Help Centre (In-App)

1. Click **Help** in the main navigation (or **?** icon).
2. Search for your issue.
3. Browse categories.
4. Click **Contact Support** if you need human help.

### Submitting a Support Ticket

1. Go to **Help → Contact Support → Submit a Ticket**.
2. Choose a category:
   - Account issue
   - Technical problem
   - Billing and payments
   - Privacy or data request
   - Report a safety issue
   - Other
3. Fill in the details:
   - Subject
   - Description (be specific)
   - Attachments (screenshots are helpful)
4. Click **Submit**.
5. You'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number.

### GitHub Issues

For developers and technical users:
- Bug reports: https://github.com/mufthakherul/Let-s-connect/issues
- Feature requests: https://github.com/mufthakherul/Let-s-connect/discussions

## 9. Response Times

Our support team aims to respond within:

| Request Type | Target Response Time |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Safety emergency (imminent harm) | Within 1 hour |
| Account hacked / security emergency | Within 4 hours |
| Account access issues | Within 24 hours |
| Content reports | Within 48–72 hours |
| General support tickets | Within 2–5 business days |
| Data rights requests (GDPR/CCPA) | Within 30 days |
| Feature requests | No guaranteed timeline |

> Response times may be longer during high-volume periods. Premium subscribers receive priority support.
