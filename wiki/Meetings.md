## 1. Meeting Overview

Milonexa meetings run directly in your browser — no app download required.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Technology** | WebRTC (browser-native) |
| **Max participants** | 50 per meeting (standard) |
| **Duration limit** | None for scheduled meetings |
| **Guest access** | Yes — share a link |
| **Screen sharing** | ✅ |
| **Recording** | ✅ (saved to your files) |
| **Chat in meetings** | ✅ |
| **Virtual backgrounds** | ✅ |
| **Noise cancellation** | ✅ |

Navigate to **Meetings** in the main navigation or at **/meetings**.

## 2. Starting a Meeting

### Instant Meeting

1. Click **Meetings → + New Meeting** (or the video icon in a group or conversation).
2. Choose a meeting title.
3. Click **Start Now**.
4. Grant camera and microphone permissions.
5. Click **Join Meeting**.
6. Share the meeting link with others.

### Quick Call from a Conversation

1. Open a DM or group conversation.
2. Click the **📹 Video Call** or **📞 Voice Call** button.
3. The other person(s) receive an incoming call notification.

---

## 3. Joining a Meeting

### Via Link

1. Click a meeting invite link.
2. The meeting pre-join screen appears.
3. Set your name (if joining as a guest) or confirm your profile name.
4. Preview your camera and audio.
5. Click **Join Meeting**.

### Via Meetings Hub

1. Go to **Meetings → Upcoming**.
2. Find the scheduled meeting.
3. Click **Join** (available 5 minutes before scheduled start time).

## 4. Meeting Controls

The meeting controls appear at the bottom of the meeting screen.

| Control | Description |
|---------|------------|
| 🎤 **Microphone** | Mute / Unmute your audio |
| 📹 **Camera** | Turn video on / off |
| 🖥️ **Share Screen** | Share your screen or a window |
| 💬 **Chat** | Open the in-meeting text chat |
| 👥 **Participants** | See who's in the meeting |
| ✋ **Raise Hand** | Signal that you want to speak |
| 🎬 **Record** | Start / stop recording |
| ⚙️ **Settings** | Audio/video settings, virtual background |
| ☎️ **Leave** | Leave the meeting |

### Muting Participants (Host)

As the meeting host:
1. Click **Participants**.
2. Click a participant's name.
3. Select **Mute** or **Ask to Unmute**.
4. Or **Mute All** to silence everyone at once.

### Removing Participants (Host)

1. **Participants → [Name] → Remove from Meeting**.
2. You can choose to prevent them from rejoining.

## 5. Screen Sharing

### Starting Screen Share

1. Click **🖥️ Share Screen** in meeting controls.
2. Choose what to share:
   - **Entire screen** — shares everything on your display
   - **A window** — shares a specific app window
   - **A browser tab** — shares a specific tab (best for presentations)
3. Click **Share**.
4. A red border appears around the shared content.

### Stopping Screen Share

Click **Stop Sharing** in the controls at the bottom, or in the share notification bar.

### Sharing Multiple Things

Only one participant can share at a time (by default). Hosts can allow simultaneous sharing in meeting settings.

## 6. Meeting Rooms

Persistent meeting rooms are always available — no scheduling needed.

### What Is a Meeting Room?

A Meeting Room has a permanent URL. Anyone with the link can join whenever the room is "open".

### Creating a Meeting Room

1. **Meetings → Rooms → + Create Room**.
2. Name the room.
3. Set a custom URL (e.g., `milonexa.com/room/team-standup`).
4. Configure access:
   - **Open** — anyone with the link can join
   - **Waiting room** — participants wait for the host to admit them
   - **Password protected** — requires a password to join
5. Click **Create Room**.

### Managing a Room

Open the room settings to:
- Lock the room (no new participants)
- Remove participants
- Change the password

## 7. Scheduling a Meeting

### Create a Scheduled Meeting

1. **Meetings → + Schedule Meeting**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**
   - **Date and time** (with timezone)
   - **Duration**
   - **Description / agenda**
   - **Invite attendees** (search for users)
   - **Repeat** (one-time, daily, weekly, monthly)
   - **Guest access** (allow external guests)
3. Click **Schedule**.

### Calendar Integration

Your scheduled meetings appear in:
- **Meetings → Upcoming** list
- Your Milonexa Calendar (Collaborate → Calendar)
- Email invites sent to attendees

### Meeting Reminders

Attendees receive:
- Email reminder (15 minutes before, by default)
- In-app notification when the meeting starts
- An in-app "Join" button available 5 minutes before

## 8. Guest Access

Invite people who don't have a Milonexa account to join a meeting.

### Sharing a Guest Link

1. In any meeting or scheduled meeting, click **Copy Invite Link**.
2. Share the link via email, message, or any other method.

### Guest Join Experience

1. Guest clicks the link.
2. They enter a display name.
3. They preview their camera and audio.
4. They join the meeting (or enter a waiting room if enabled).

> Guests have limited features — they can use camera, microphone, and chat but cannot host or manage participants.

## 9. Recording Meetings

### Starting a Recording

1. In the meeting controls, click **🎬 Record**.
2. All participants are notified that recording has started.
3. The record button turns red.

### Stopping Recording

1. Click **🎬 Stop Recording**.
2. The recording is processed (may take a few minutes).
3. You receive a notification when it's ready.

### Accessing Recordings

**Meetings → Recordings** — see all recordings you've made.

From here you can:
- Watch the recording
- Download it (MP4 format)
- Share it with others (generates a view link)
- Delete it

### Recording Consent

- All participants must be notified before recording (this is done automatically by the platform)
- Always get consent from participants before recording — this may be a legal requirement in your jurisdiction

## 10. Meeting Settings

### Before the Meeting (Pre-join Screen)

Configure your audio and video before joining:
- Choose camera and microphone from dropdowns
- Set a **virtual background** (blur, or upload an image)
- Test your audio

### During the Meeting (⚙️ Settings)

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Camera** | Select device, enable virtual background |
| **Microphone** | Select device, enable noise cancellation |
| **Speaker** | Select output device |
| **Quality** | Adjust video quality to match connection speed |

### Meeting Privacy

When scheduling, set who can join:
- **Anyone with the link**
- **Invited attendees only** (login required)
- **Waiting room** (host must admit each participant)
