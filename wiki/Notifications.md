## 1. Types of Notifications

Milonexa notifies you about the following activities:

### Social Activity

| Notification | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| **New like/reaction** | Someone reacts to your post or comment |
| **New comment** | Someone comments on your post |
| **Comment reply** | Someone replies to your comment |
| **New follower** | Someone follows your account |
| **Friend request** | Someone sends you a friend request |
| **Friend request accepted** | Your friend request was accepted |
| **Tag in post** | You're tagged in someone's post |
| **Mention** | Someone @mentions you in a post or comment |
| **Post shared** | Someone shares or reposts your content |

### Messaging

| Notification | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| **New message** | New DM received |
| **Group message** | New message in a group conversation |
| **Server message** | New message in a server channel you're active in |
| **@mention in server** | Someone @mentions you in a server channel |

### Groups & Communities

| Notification | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| **Group invite** | Invited to join a group |
| **Join request approved** | Your request to join a group was accepted |
| **Group post** | New post in a group you're in (opt-in) |
| **Admin activity** | Changes to groups you admin |

### Marketplace & Commerce

| Notification | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| **Order placed** | You've placed an order |
| **Order shipped** | Your order has shipped |
| **Order delivered** | Your order has been delivered |
| **New order (seller)** | Someone ordered your product |
| **New review** | Someone reviewed your product or profile |

### Platform

| Notification | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| **Content moderation** | A post or comment was removed |
| **Account security alert** | New login from unrecognised device |
| **System announcements** | Platform-wide news and updates |

## 2. Viewing Notifications

### Notification Bell

The 🔔 bell icon in the navigation bar shows your unread notification count.

1. Click the 🔔 bell to open the **Notification Panel**.
2. Notifications are sorted by most recent.
3. Click a notification to navigate to the relevant content.
4. Click **Mark all as read** to clear the badge.

### Full Notification Page

For a full history of all notifications:
- Click **See all notifications** at the bottom of the panel, or
- Navigate directly to **/notifications**

The notification page lets you:
- Filter by type (All, Reactions, Comments, Follows, Messages, etc.)
- Mark individual or all notifications as read
- Delete individual notifications

## 3. Notification Settings

Access all notification settings at **Settings → Notifications**.

Settings are grouped by:
- **In-app** — notifications inside the platform
- **Push** — browser or mobile device notifications
- **Email** — email notifications

For each activity type, you can choose:

| Option | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **On** | Always notify |
| **Off** | Never notify |
| **Friends only** | Only notify when the action is from a friend |

## 4. Push Notifications (Browser & Mobile)

### Enabling Browser Push Notifications

1. Click the 🔔 bell in the navigation.
2. Click **Enable push notifications** (or go to Settings → Notifications → Push Notifications).
3. Your browser will ask permission — click **Allow**.
4. Push notifications are now active even when the browser tab is closed.

> Push notifications work best when Milonexa is installed as a PWA. See [Mobile & PWA](Mobile-and-PWA).

### Enabling Mobile Push (PWA)

1. Install Milonexa as a PWA on your device (see [Mobile & PWA](Mobile-and-PWA)).
2. When prompted, allow notifications.
3. Configure which notifications you want via **Settings → Notifications → Push**.

### Disabling Push Notifications

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Push Notifications**.
2. Toggle **Enable Push Notifications** to Off.

Or use your browser's site settings to revoke notification permission.

## 5. Email Notifications

### Available Email Notifications

| Email Type | Default | Description |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Account security** | On | Login from new device, password changed |
| **Direct messages** | On | When you're offline and receive a DM |
| **Friend requests** | On | New friend request received |
| **Reactions & comments** | Off | Activity on your posts |
| **New followers** | Off | When someone follows you |
| **Group activity** | Off | Activity in your groups |
| **Marketplace orders** | On | Order and shipping updates |
| **Platform announcements** | On | Major platform news |
| **Weekly digest** | Off | Weekly summary of activity |

### Managing Email Notifications

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Email**.
2. Toggle each category on or off.
3. Click **Save**.

### Unsubscribe from All

Every email sent by Milonexa includes an **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom. Clicking it disables all marketing and digest emails while keeping critical security emails active.

## 6. In-App Notification Preferences

Fine-tune which notifications appear inside the app.

### By Category

Go to **Settings → Notifications → In-App** and configure:

- 🤝 **Social:** Reactions, comments, friend requests, follows, mentions
- 💬 **Messages:** DMs, group chats, server mentions
- 👥 **Groups:** Posts, admin activity, join approvals
- 🛒 **Shopping:** Order updates, seller activity
- 🔒 **Security:** Login alerts, account changes

### Notification Sound

Enable or disable the notification sound:
- **Settings → Notifications → Sounds**

You can also choose different sound profiles for different notification types.

## 7. Muting Notifications

### Mute a Specific Post

Stop getting notifications about a post you've commented on or reacted to:
1. Click ⋮ on the post.
2. Select **Turn off notifications for this post**.

### Mute a Person

Stop getting notifications from a specific user:
1. Visit their profile.
2. Click ⋮ → **Mute notifications from @username**.

Muting stops notifications but still lets them interact with your content.

### Mute a Group

Reduce or stop notifications from a specific group:
1. In the group, click the notification bell icon (or ⋮).
2. Choose: **All Notifications**, **Highlights Only**, or **Muted**.

### Do Not Disturb Mode

Set a global **Do Not Disturb** period to pause all notifications:
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Do Not Disturb**.
2. Enable and set:
   - Start time
   - End time
   - Whether to allow urgent notifications through

## 8. Notification Digest

Instead of individual notifications, get a single summary:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Digest**.
2. Enable the digest.
3. Choose frequency: **Hourly**, **Daily**, or **Weekly**.
4. Choose delivery: **In-app**, **Email**, or **Both**.

Digest mode batches similar notifications (e.g., "15 people reacted to your post") instead of sending one notification per reaction.
