## 1. Search Basics

The 🔍 **search bar** is always visible at the top of the page.

### How to Search

1. Click the search bar (or press `/` on your keyboard as a shortcut).
2. Type your query.
3. As you type, **instant suggestions** appear — click one to jump directly.
4. Press **Enter** or click the search icon to see full results.

### Search Query Tips

| Tip | Example |
|-----|---------|
| Use **keywords** for general topics | `react javascript` |
| Use **@username** to find a specific user | `@johndoe` |
| Use **#hashtag** to search a topic | `#photography` |
| Use **"quotes"** for exact phrases | `"climate change policy"` |
| Use **location** to filter geographically | `coffee shops London` |

## 2. Filtering Search Results

After searching, use the **filter bar** below the search box to refine results:

| Filter Tab | What It Shows |
|-----------|--------------|
| **All** | Best-matching results across all types |
| **People** | User profiles and pages |
| **Posts** | Public posts and articles |
| **Groups** | Public and restricted groups |
| **Videos** | Video content |
| **Photos** | Image posts and albums |
| **Pages** | Creator and business pages |
| **Events** | Upcoming and recent events |
| **Marketplace** | Products for sale |

### Advanced Filters

Within each tab, additional filters may include:

- **Date range:** Today, this week, this month, custom range
- **Location:** Filter by city, country
- **Language:** Content in a specific language
- **Posted by:** Anyone, Friends, Pages you follow

## 3. Searching for People

Find users by name, username, or interests.

### People Search

1. Search for a name or @username.
2. Click the **People** filter.
3. Results show name, username, mutual friends, and a brief bio.

### Profile Preview

Hover over (or click) any result to see a quick profile preview without leaving the search page. From the preview you can:
- Follow
- Send a friend request
- View their full profile

### Finding People You Know

Use **Find People You Know** to discover connections:
1. Go to **Friends → Find People**.
2. Browse suggestions from your contact list, mutual friends, or interests.

## 4. Searching for Posts & Content

### Posts Filter

In the **Posts** filter, results are sorted by:
- **Most relevant** (default)
- **Most recent**
- **Most popular** (highest engagement)

### Searching Within a Profile

To search posts from a specific user:
1. Go to their profile.
2. Click the 🔍 icon (top-right of their profile, or a Search Posts option).
3. Type keywords to search within their posts only.

### Searching Within a Group

1. Open the group.
2. Click the 🔍 search icon in the group's top bar.
3. Search within that group's posts only.

## 5. Searching for Groups

1. Use the main search bar.
2. Select the **Groups** filter.
3. Groups are displayed with:
   - Group name and cover image
   - Member count
   - Privacy type (Public / Private)
   - Short description

### Group Category Browse

You can also discover groups without searching:
1. Go to **Groups → Discover**.
2. Browse by category: Technology, Fitness, Gaming, Travel, Education, Art, Business, etc.
3. See featured groups curated by Milonexa.

## 6. Searching for Videos & Media

### Video Search

1. Search for a topic.
2. Select the **Videos** filter.
3. Results include uploaded videos and (for streaming) on-demand content.

Filters available for videos:
- **Duration:** Short (<5 min), Medium (5–20 min), Long (>20 min)
- **Date posted:** Today, this week, this month
- **Quality:** HD, 4K

### Media Search within Your Content

Find your own photos and videos quickly:
1. Go to **Profile → Photos** or **Profile → Videos**.
2. Use the search bar within that section.

## 7. Hashtag Pages

Every hashtag has its own page — a feed of all posts using that tag.

### Visiting a Hashtag Page

- Click any `#hashtag` in a post, comment, or bio
- Or search `#hashtag` in the search bar

### Hashtag Page Features

- **Recent posts** feed using the hashtag
- **Top posts** (most engaged posts with this tag)
- **Follow the hashtag** — posts with this tag appear in your feed
- **Related hashtags** — discover similar topics

### Trending Hashtags

See what's trending right now:
- Check the **Trending** sidebar on the homepage and Explore page
- Top 10 trending hashtags are updated hourly

## 8. Trending Content

### What Makes Content Trend?

Milonexa calculates trending based on:
- Rapid increase in engagement (reactions, comments, shares)
- Volume of posts about a topic within a short window
- Geographic popularity (global vs. local trending)

### Local vs. Global Trending

- **Local trending:** Topics popular in your region
- **Global trending:** Topics popular platform-wide

Switch between local and global trending using the toggle on the Explore or Trending page.

## 10. Search History & Saved Searches

### Search History

Milonexa saves your recent searches for quick access.

- Recent searches appear as suggestions when you click the search bar
- Clear individual searches by clicking the ✕ next to each
- Clear all history: Search bar → **Clear all recent searches**

### Disable Search History

Turn off search history: **Settings → Privacy → Search History → Off**.

When disabled, searches are not saved and won't appear as suggestions.

### Saved Searches

Save a search to quickly re-run it later:
1. Run a search.
2. Click **⋮ → Save this search** (in the search results header).
3. Find all saved searches under **Profile → Saved Searches**.

Saved searches can have **alerts** enabled — you'll be notified when new content matches.
