## 3. Buying a Product

1. Find a product you want to buy.
2. Select any options (size, colour, quantity).

4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout.

- Click **🔖 Save** (adds to your bookmarks / wishlist)

1. **Shop → Wishlist → + Create Wishlist**
2. Add products to the wishlist.
3. Share the link with family and friends.

## 4. Cart & Checkout

### Your Cart

View your cart: **Shop → Cart** or the 🛒 icon in the navigation.

The cart shows:
- All items with quantity, price, and variant selected
- Shipping estimate
- Subtotal, tax, and total

### Checkout Steps

1. Review items in your cart.
2. Click **Proceed to Checkout**.
3. Enter / confirm **shipping address**.
4. Choose **shipping method** (if multiple offered).
5. Enter a **discount code** (if you have one).
6. Review the **order summary**.
7. Enter **payment details** (card via Stripe).
8. Click **Place Order**.

### Payment Security

- All payments are processed by **Stripe** — a globally trusted payment provider.
- Milonexa never stores your full card number.
- Payments are secured by SSL/TLS encryption.
- Look for the padlock 🔒 in your browser's address bar.

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Apple Pay, Google Pay (where supported by browser)
- Other methods vary by region

## 5. Orders & Tracking

### Viewing Your Orders

**Profile → Orders** (or **Shop → Your Orders**) shows all your purchases with:
- Order number and date
- Items ordered
- Total paid
- Current order status
- Tracking information (when available)

### Order Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Payment received** | Order confirmed, awaiting seller preparation |
| **Processing** | Seller is preparing your order |
| **Shipped** | Your order is on its way |
| **Out for delivery** | With the delivery carrier |
| **Delivered** | Marked as delivered |
| **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled |
| **Refund processing** | Refund has been initiated |
| **Refunded** | Refund complete |

### Tracking Shipments

When your order ships, you'll receive:
- A notification on Milonexa
- An email with tracking details
- A tracking number and link in your Order Details

Click the tracking number to see real-time delivery updates.

## 6. Returns & Refunds

### Requesting a Return

1. Go to **Profile → Orders → [Order] → Request Return/Refund**.
2. Select the items you want to return.
3. Choose a reason:
   - Item not as described
   - Item damaged on arrival
   - Wrong item received
   - Changed my mind
4. Add photos if relevant (especially for damaged items).
5. Submit the request.

The seller has **3 business days** to respond.

### Refund Timeline

| Method | Timeline |
|--------|---------|
| Original payment method | 5–10 business days after approval |
| Platform credit | 1–2 business days |

### Buyer Protection

If a seller does not respond to a return request or you cannot resolve the issue:
1. Escalate to **Milonexa Dispute Resolution**: Order → **Open Dispute**.
2. Provide evidence (screenshots, photos).
3. Milonexa's team reviews and makes a decision within **7 business days**.

## 7. Selling on Milonexa

### Setting Up Your Shop

1. Go to **Shop → Sell → Set Up Your Shop**.
2. Enter your **shop name** and description.
3. Upload a shop logo and banner.
4. Enter **payout information** (bank account or PayPal via Stripe Connect).
5. Set your **return and refund policy**.
6. Click **Open Shop**.

### Listing a Product

1. **Your Shop → + Add Product**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** (clear, descriptive)
   - **Description** (detailed specs, sizes, materials, etc.)
   - **Photos** (up to 8 images; first image is the thumbnail)
   - **Category**
   - **Price** (and optional compare-at price for discounts)
   - **Stock quantity**
   - **Variants** (size, colour, etc. — optional)
   - **SKU / Barcode** (optional, for your inventory)
   - **Shipping settings** (methods, costs, estimated delivery)
   - **Digital download** (if applicable — upload the file)
3. Click **Publish** to make it live.

### Product Photos

Good photos significantly increase sales:
- Use natural light
- Show the product from multiple angles
- Include size reference
- Show the product in use
- Minimum: 1 photo. Recommended: 4–8 photos.

## 8. Managing Your Shop

### Shop Dashboard

**Shop → Your Shop → Dashboard** shows:
- Total sales and revenue
- Active listings
- Pending orders
- Recent reviews
- Low-stock alerts

### Processing Orders

When a buyer orders:
1. You receive a notification.
2. Go to **Your Shop → Orders**.
3. Prepare and ship the order.
4. Mark as **Shipped** and enter tracking details.

### Inventory Management

Set stock quantities per product. When stock reaches **0**, the product shows as "Out of Stock" and can't be purchased.

Enable **Low Stock Alerts** in Shop Settings to get notified when stock drops below a threshold.

### Shop Holidays / Pause

If you need a break:
1. **Shop → Settings → Shop Holiday Mode**.
2. Enable with a return date.
3. Products remain listed but show "On Holiday" — buyers can't place orders.

## 9. Product Reviews

### Leaving a Review

After receiving an order:
1. Go to **Orders → [Order] → Leave Review**.
2. Rate the product (1–5 stars).
3. Write a review (optional but helpful).
4. Add photos of the product (optional).
5. Submit.

You have **60 days** after the order is delivered to leave a review.

### Responding to Reviews (Sellers)

Sellers can respond to reviews:
1. **Your Shop → Reviews → [Review] → Reply**.
2. Keep responses professional and constructive.

## 10. Marketplace Safety

### Safe Shopping Tips

- ✅ Check seller ratings and reviews before buying
- ✅ Read the product description thoroughly
- ✅ Review the return policy before purchasing
- ✅ Pay through Milonexa's checkout (never off-platform)
- ❌ Never pay via bank transfer, gift cards, or cryptocurrency to a seller
- ❌ Don't share personal financial details via messages

### Reporting a Problem

**Report a suspicious listing:**
1. On the product page → ⋮ → **Report Listing**
2. Choose a reason and submit

**Report a seller:**
1. Seller profile → ⋮ → **Report Seller**

### Fraud Protection

- Milonexa monitors for fraudulent sellers and listings
- Suspicious activity may result in listings being removed and accounts suspended
- Never complete purchases outside the platform — buyer protection only applies to in-platform transactions
