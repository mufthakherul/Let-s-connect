## 1. Your Social Feed

Navigate to **/feed** to see your personalised social feed.

### What Appears in Your Feed

| Content Source | Why It Appears |
|---------------|---------------|
| Friends' posts | Posts from your mutual friends |
| Followed accounts | Posts from people you follow |
| Group posts | Public posts from groups you've joined |
| Recommended posts | AI-suggested content based on your interests |
| Sponsored content | Clearly labelled promotional posts |
| Trending topics | Popular content from the wider community |

### Feed Filters

Use the filter bar at the top of the feed to narrow by:
- **All** — everything (default)
- **Friends** — posts from friends only
- **Following** — posts from followed accounts
- **Groups** — posts from your groups
- **Photos** — image posts only
- **Videos** — video posts only

### Refresh & Real-Time Updates

The feed updates in real time. A "New posts" notification bar appears at the top when new content is available — click it to refresh without losing your scroll position.

## 2. Creating Posts

### Quick Compose

Click the **"What's on your mind?"** box at the top of your feed or the ✏️ icon in the navigation.

### Post Types

| Type | How to Create |
|------|-------------|
| **Text post** | Just type in the compose box |
| **Photo post** | Click the 📷 camera icon, upload up to 4 images |
| **Video post** | Click the 🎥 video icon, upload a video file |
| **Link post** | Paste a URL — a preview card appears automatically |
| **Poll** | Click ☑️ → add options and duration |
| **File post** | Click 📎 → attach up to 3 files |
| **Blog post** | Click ✍️ Blog → opens the rich text editor |

### Supported Media

| Media | Formats | Max Size | Notes |
|-------|---------|---------|-------|
| Images | JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF | 10 MB each | Up to 4 per post |
| Videos | MP4, WebM, MOV | 100 MB | Up to 10 minutes; auto-transcoded |
| Files | PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, ZIP, etc. | 50 MB each | Up to 3 per post |

### Rich Text Features

In the compose box and blog editor:
- **Bold** with `**text**` or Ctrl+B
- *Italic* with `*text*` or Ctrl+I
- Links with `[text](url)` or Ctrl+K
- @mention users with `@username`
- Hashtags with `#topic`
- Emoji from the 😊 picker

### Scheduling Posts

You can schedule posts to publish at a future date and time:
1. In the compose window, click the **⏰ Schedule** button.
2. Set the date and time.
3. Click **Schedule Post**.

Scheduled posts appear under **Profile → Scheduled** and can be edited or cancelled.

## 3. Post Visibility & Audience

Each post can have its own visibility setting:

| Level | Who Can See |
|-------|------------|
| 🌍 **Public** | Everyone, including non-logged-in visitors and search engines |
| 👥 **Friends** | Only your mutual friends |
| 🔒 **Private** | Only you |
| 👁️ **Custom** | Specific friends or groups you select |

Change visibility per post in the compose window, or set a default in **Settings → Privacy → Default Post Visibility**.

> 💡 You can edit the visibility of existing posts at any time from the post's **⋮ More Options** menu.

## 4. Reactions & Engagement

### React to Posts

Hover (or long-press on mobile) over the **👍 Like** button to see all reactions:

| Reaction | Emoji | Meaning |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Like | 👍 | General approval |
| Love | ❤️ | Strong positive reaction |
| Laugh | 😂 | Funny content |
| Wow | 😮 | Surprising or impressive |
| Sad | 😢 | Sad or sympathetic |
| Angry | 😡 | Disagreement or outrage |

Click a reaction to apply it. Click the same reaction again to remove it.

### Post Metrics

Each post shows:
- Total reaction count (with breakdown on hover)
- Number of comments
- Number of reposts/shares
- View count (for videos)

## 5. Comments & Discussions

### Commenting

Click **Comment** below a post to open the comment box. Comments support:
- Text (up to 2,000 characters)
- @mentions
- Emojis
- GIFs (via built-in GIF picker)
- Images (single image per comment)
- Replies to specific comments (nested threading)

### Reactions on Comments

You can react to individual comments the same way you react to posts.

### Comment Management

**As the post author**, you can:
- Pin a comment to the top
- Turn off comments on your post (⋮ → Turn off commenting)
- Delete any comment on your post
- Hide comments from specific users

**As a commenter**, you can:
- Edit your comment (within 15 minutes of posting)
- Delete your comment at any time

## 6. Reposts & Sharing

### Repost

Share someone else's post to your followers while keeping the original attribution:
1. Click the **🔁 Repost** button below the post.
2. Add an optional comment on top.
3. Choose visibility.
4. Click **Repost**.

### Share

Copy a link or share to other platforms:
1. Click **↗ Share** → **Copy Link** or choose a platform.
2. The post URL is copied to your clipboard.

### Saving vs. Sharing

- **Bookmark** (🔖): Save privately for yourself — see [Bookmarks](Bookmarks)
- **Share**: Distribute to others
- **Repost**: Re-publish to your feed with attribution

## 7. Friends System

### Sending a Friend Request

1. Visit the user's profile.
2. Click **Add Friend** (or the 👤+ icon).
3. They receive a friend request notification.
4. When they accept, you become mutual friends.

### Managing Friend Requests

Go to **Friends → Friend Requests** to see pending requests. You can:
- **Accept** — become friends
- **Decline** — reject without notification
- **Delete request** — withdraw a request you sent

### Unfriending

1. Go to the user's profile.
2. Click **Friends → Unfriend**.
3. They are removed from your friends list (and you from theirs). No notification is sent.

### Friend Suggestions

Milonexa suggests friends based on:
- Mutual friends
- People in the same groups
- Shared interests and topics
- Similar location (if location is enabled)

Find suggestions under **Friends → People You May Know**.

## 8. Following

Following is one-directional — you can follow someone without them following you back.

### Follow a User

1. Visit their profile.
2. Click **Follow**.
3. Their posts will appear in your feed.

For **private accounts**, clicking Follow sends a follow request. They must approve it.

### Unfollow

1. Visit their profile.
2. Click **Following → Unfollow**.
3. Their posts will no longer appear in your feed. They are not notified.

### Followers vs. Following

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Followers** | People who follow you |
| **Following** | People you follow |
| **Friends** | Mutual connections (both follow each other + friend connection) |

## 10. Skills & Endorsements

Your profile can display skills you have (e.g., "Graphic Design", "Python", "Marketing").

### Adding Skills

1. Go to **Profile → Edit → Skills**.
2. Type a skill and press Enter.
3. Add up to **50 skills**.
4. Click **Save**.

### Endorsing Someone

When you visit a friend's profile, you can endorse their listed skills:
1. Scroll to the Skills section.
2. Click the ✅ **Endorse** button next to a skill.
3. The endorsement count for that skill increases.

Endorsements come from trusted connections and add credibility to your profile.

## 11. Stories & Moments

Stories are short-form content that disappears after **24 hours**.

### Creating a Story

1. Click your profile picture with the **+** icon at the top of your feed.
2. Take a photo, record a short video (up to 60 seconds), or select from your camera roll.
3. Add stickers, text, or drawings.
4. Set the audience (Friends / Close Friends / Public).
5. Post.

### Viewing Stories

Stories appear in a horizontal row at the top of your feed. Tap/click a circle to watch.

### Story Privacy

Stories respect your privacy settings. Use **Close Friends** to share with a curated list:
1. Go to **Settings → Privacy → Close Friends List**.
2. Search for and add friends to your close friends list.

## 12. Feed Algorithm

Milonexa's feed uses AI to rank and personalise content. Here's how it works:

### Ranking Signals

| Signal | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Recency** | High | Newer posts rank higher |
| **Your engagement** | High | Topics/people you interact with most |
| **Post engagement** | Medium | Likes, comments, shares on the post |
| **Relationship strength** | High | Close friends appear more often |
| **Content type preference** | Medium | If you tend to watch videos, more videos |
| **Reported content** | Negative | Reported posts are down-ranked |

### Controlling Your Feed

- **Snooze** someone for 30 days: ⋮ on their post → Snooze @username
- **See less like this**: ⋮ → See less of this type
- **Unfollow** to remove entirely
- **Interests**: Settings → Feed → Manage Interests

## 13. Hashtags & Mentions

### Hashtags `#topic`

Add hashtags to your posts to make them discoverable:
- Use `#technology`, `#travel`, `#food`, etc.
- Hashtags are searchable — your post appears in that hashtag's feed
- Up to **30 hashtags** per post (but 3–5 relevant ones perform better)
- Browse trending hashtags on the **Explore** page

### Mentions `@username`

Tag other users in posts, comments, or captions:
- Type `@` followed by their username
- The user receives a notification that they've been mentioned
- Their username appears as a clickable link

### Mentioned by Others

You'll receive a notification when someone mentions you. To manage who can mention you: **Settings → Notifications → Mentions**.
