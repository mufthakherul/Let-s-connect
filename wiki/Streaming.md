## 2. Live TV

### What's Available

Milonexa's IPTV directory includes live channels across categories:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **News** | International news networks, regional news |
| **Sports** | Football, cricket, basketball, motorsports |
| **Entertainment** | TV shows, reality, game shows |
| **Movies** | Film channels, classic movies |
| **Kids** | Children's cartoons, educational |
| **Music** | Music video channels |
| **Documentary** | Nature, history, science |
| **Religious** | Faith-based channels |
| **Local/Regional** | Local broadcast TV by country |

### Browsing Live TV

1. Go to **Streaming → Live TV**.
2. Browse by:
   - **Category** — filter by genre
   - **Country** — channels from a specific country
   - **Language** — channels in a specific language
   - **Search** — type a channel name
3. Click a channel to start watching.

### Electronic Programme Guide (EPG)

Many channels include a live programme guide showing:
- What's on now
- What's coming up next
- Show descriptions

Look for the 📋 **Guide** icon on supported channels.

## 3. Internet Radio

### What's Available

- 10,000+ radio stations from around the world
- All genres: Pop, Rock, Hip-Hop, Jazz, Classical, Electronic, Country, R&B, Talk, News, Sports, etc.
- Multilingual stations

### Listening to Radio

1. Go to **Streaming → Radio**.
2. Browse by genre, country, or language.
3. Use the **search bar** to find a specific station.
4. Click a station to start listening.

### Radio Player

The radio player appears at the bottom of the screen and continues playing as you navigate the platform — no need to stay on the radio page.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| ▶️ / ⏸ | Play / Pause |
| 🔊 | Volume slider |
| ❤️ | Favourite the station |
| ℹ️ | Station info (genre, bitrate, website) |

## 4. Finding Channels & Stations

### Search

1. In the Streaming section, use the **🔍 Search** bar.
2. Type the channel name, station name, or genre.
3. Select from results.

### Featured & Curated

- **Featured channels** — Milonexa's curated selection of popular channels
- **Trending** — channels with the most viewers right now
- **New additions** — recently added channels and stations

### Browse by Country

1. Click **By Country** in the streaming navigation.
2. Select a continent, then country.
3. See all channels and stations from that country.

## 5. Favourites & Watchlists

### Favourite a Channel or Station

1. Click the ❤️ **Favourite** icon next to any channel or station.
2. It's added to your **Favourites** list.

### Accessing Favourites

Go to **Streaming → Favourites** to see all your saved channels and stations.

### Recently Watched / Listened

**Streaming → History** shows your recently watched channels and listened stations. Clear history at any time from this page.

### Continue Watching

Milonexa remembers where you left off for channels that support it. Look for **Continue** indicators.

## 6. Quality & Playback

### Video Quality

Live TV streams at the quality provided by the channel (typically 480p or 720p, some channels at 1080p). Quality selection is available on channels that offer multiple streams:

1. In the TV player: ⚙️ → **Quality**.
2. Choose from available options.

### Buffering Issues

If a stream buffers frequently:
- Try a lower quality setting
- Check your internet connection speed (streaming needs at least 5 Mbps for HD)
- Try refreshing the page
- Some channels may have geographic restrictions — try a different channel from the same region

### Audio-Only Mode

To save data while listening, switch to audio-only:
- In the player: ⚙️ → **Audio Only Mode**.
- Useful for radio and news channels.

### Picture-in-Picture

Keep the stream playing while browsing:
1. In the player: click the **⧉ Picture-in-Picture** button.
2. The player shrinks to a corner of your screen.
3. You can drag and resize it.

## 7. Streaming FAQs

**Q: Is streaming really free?**  
A: Yes. The IPTV directory and radio stations are free to access with any Milonexa account.

**Q: Why can't I find my local channel?**  
A: The directory includes user-contributed streams. Some local channels may not be in the database yet. You can suggest additions via the **Suggest a Channel** option in the streaming section.

**Q: A channel shows but won't play. Why?**  
A: Channel availability can vary based on your geographic location, stream uptime, and broadcaster restrictions. Try refreshing, or browse alternative channels in the same category.

**Q: Is content downloaded for offline viewing?**  
A: Live streams are not available offline. The platform requires an active internet connection for streaming.

**Q: Are there age restrictions on any channels?**  
A: Some channels are marked as adult content and are behind an age gate. You must have date of birth set in your account to access age-restricted channels.

**Q: Can I cast to my TV?**  
A: Yes — browsers that support Chromecast (Chrome) or AirPlay (Safari) will show the cast button in the player. Click it to send the stream to your TV.
