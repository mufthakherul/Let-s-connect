## 1. Eligibility & Registration

### Age Requirements

| Region | Minimum Age |
|--------|------------|
| Global default | **13 years old** |
| European Union (GDPR) | **16 years old** (or 13 with verified parental consent) |
| United States (COPPA) | **13 years old** |

> If we discover an account belonging to someone below the minimum age, it will be suspended and all data removed.

### One Account Per Person

Each person may hold **one personal account**. You may also create:
- Up to **5 Pages** (public creator/business pages)
- One **Developer account** (for API access)

Multiple personal accounts are not permitted except as explicitly allowed by these guidelines.

### Accurate Information

You must provide accurate information when registering. Providing false identity information, using another person's identity, or creating accounts to deceive is prohibited.

## 2. Account Responsibility

You are fully responsible for everything that happens through your account.

### What You Are Responsible For

- All content you post, share, or upload
- All messages sent from your account
- All interactions with other users
- Keeping your password secure and confidential
- Logging out of shared or public computers
- Enabling 2FA to prevent unauthorised access

### Sharing Access

> Do not share your account credentials with anyone.

If you need multiple people to manage a **Page** or **Group**, use the admin roles feature — never share your personal login.

### Notification of Compromise

If you believe your account has been accessed by someone else:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Revoke all active sessions (Settings → Security)
3. Contact support

## 3. Acceptable Use

Milonexa is provided for **lawful, personal, and commercial use** as described in these guidelines.

### ✅ Permitted Uses

- Connecting and communicating with others
- Creating, sharing, and discovering content
- Building communities around shared interests
- Selling and buying products via the Marketplace
- Collaborating on documents and projects
- Accessing public streams and media
- Developing applications using the public API

### ❌ Prohibited Uses

- Any use that violates applicable laws or regulations
- Attempting to access accounts or data you are not authorised to access
- Reverse-engineering platform security features
- Using the platform to plan or commit criminal acts
- Scraping data at scale without explicit API authorisation
- Using automated tools to perform actions faster than humanly possible (without API approval)
- Attempting to degrade, disrupt, or attack the platform's infrastructure

## 5. Interactions with Other Users

### Respectful Communication

All communication on Milonexa — public posts, comments, messages, or in groups — must comply with the [Community Guidelines](Community-Guidelines).

### Consent in Communication

- Do not send unsolicited bulk messages
- Respect users' messaging preferences (some users only accept messages from friends)
- If someone asks you to stop contacting them, you must stop

### Connections & Following

- You can follow any public account without approval
- Private profiles require the account holder to approve follow requests
- Friend requests require mutual acceptance
- Do not attempt to manipulate someone's follower or connection counts through automated tools

## 6. Platform Integrity

### No Manipulation of Metrics

Artificial inflation of engagement metrics is prohibited:
- Buying or selling likes, reactions, shares, followers, or comments
- Using bots, scripts, or automation to like, share, follow, or comment
- Coordinating inauthentic engagement in exchange for reciprocal engagement ("pods")
- Creating fake accounts to vote in polls or upvote content

### Authentic Representation

- Your account must represent your real identity or a clearly labelled persona
- Impersonating real people, celebrities, brands, or organisations is prohibited
- Parody accounts must clearly state they are parody in their bio
- Verified badge claims require identity verification

### Security of the Platform

Do not:
- Attempt to probe, scan, or test the security of Milonexa systems
- Upload malware, viruses, or any harmful code
- Use cross-site scripting (XSS), injection attacks, or similar exploitation techniques
- Interfere with other users' access to the platform
- Exploit bugs for personal gain (instead, report them responsibly — see [Help & Support](Help-and-Support))

> 🐛 **Found a security vulnerability?** Please report it responsibly through our **Security Disclosure** channel rather than exploiting it. We appreciate responsible disclosure.

## 7. Commercial & Promotional Activity

### Promotional Posts & Advertising

You may promote your business, products, or services on Milonexa, subject to these rules:
- Promotions must be honest and not misleading
- Sponsored or paid promotions must be clearly labelled (`#ad`, `#sponsored`, or `#paid`)
- You may not promote products or services that are illegal or violate these guidelines
- Pyramid schemes, multi-level marketing (MLM) recruitment spam, and fraudulent schemes are prohibited

### Affiliate Links

Posting affiliate links is permitted if:
- You clearly disclose that the link is an affiliate link
- The product or service is legitimate and not prohibited by these guidelines

### Business Accounts

Businesses and organisations are welcome on Milonexa. Use a **Page** (not a personal account) to represent a business. This allows fans to follow without becoming mutual friends.

## 8. Third-Party Services & Integrations

### Connecting External Apps

You can connect third-party apps to your Milonexa account (e.g., for posting, analytics, or automation).

- Only connect apps you trust
- Review and understand what permissions each app requests
- Revoke access to apps you no longer use (Settings → Security → Connected Apps)
- You are responsible for any content posted via connected apps

### Third-Party Content Links

You may share links to external websites and content. However:
- Do not link to malicious websites, malware, or phishing pages
- Do not link to content that would itself violate these guidelines if posted directly
- Milonexa is not responsible for the content of external sites

## 9. Marketplace Rules

### For Sellers

- List only legal products and services
- Provide accurate product descriptions, pricing, and images
- Ship orders within stated timeframes
- Respond to buyer queries within 3 business days
- Issue refunds as required by applicable consumer protection laws

### For Buyers

- Make purchases in good faith
- Contact the seller before initiating a dispute
- Use the platform's dispute resolution process fairly
- Do not file fraudulent chargebacks

### Prohibited Marketplace Items

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Illegal goods | Drugs, weapons without required licensing |
| Counterfeit items | Fake branded goods, pirated software |
| Stolen property | Any items obtained through theft |
| Dangerous goods | Items that present unreasonable safety risks |
| Harmful services | Hacking services, fake followers, academic fraud |
| Digital piracy | Cracked software, leaked media, unlicensed streaming keys |

## 10. Live Streaming & Video Guidelines

### Content Standards

Live streams and uploaded videos must comply with all content rules in the [Community Guidelines](Community-Guidelines).

Additional rules specific to video and streaming:

- Do not broadcast accidents, violence, or harm without responsible context
- Do not stream other people's copyrighted content without permission
- Age-restricted content must be appropriately gated
- Do not use misleading thumbnails or titles (clickbait) that misrepresent content

### Copyright in Video

- Do not use copyrighted music, clips, or other media without a licence
- Content ID-style matching may automatically flag or mute copyrighted audio
- Repeated copyright violations may result in your upload ability being revoked

## 11. Groups & Communities Rules

### Group Admins & Moderators

Group creators and admins are responsible for their community:
- Set and enforce clear group rules
- Moderate content to comply with Milonexa's guidelines
- Remove members who violate guidelines
- Do not use admin powers to harass or target members

### Private vs. Public Groups

| Type | Who Can See | Who Can Join |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Public** | Anyone | Anyone |
| **Restricted** | Anyone | Must request to join |
| **Private (Secret)** | Members only | Invited by existing members |

Members of private groups must not share group content or member information publicly.

## 12. Collaboration & Document Rules

When using collaborative documents, wikis, and project boards:

- Do not vandalise shared documents belonging to others
- Respect document ownership and sharing permissions set by the owner
- Do not use collaboration features to store or share prohibited content
- You may share collaborative spaces with other users, but the creator remains responsible for the content

## 13. API & Developer Usage

Developers using the Milonexa API must comply with additional terms:

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Authentication** | All requests must use valid OAuth tokens |
| **Rate limits** | Respect rate limits; do not attempt to bypass them |
| **User data** | Only request data your app needs; minimise data retention |
| **No scraping** | Bulk data collection outside the API is prohibited |
| **Attribution** | Apps must identify themselves in request headers |
| **Terms compliance** | Apps built on the API must enforce these guidelines for their users |

API documentation: [API Lifecycle](API-Lifecycle)

## 14. Consequences of Violations

Milonexa enforces these guidelines through a graduated system:

| Severity | Examples | Response |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 🟡 **Minor** | Minor spam, accidental repost of mildly prohibited content | Warning, content removal |
| 🟠 **Moderate** | Repeated spam, harassment, misinformation | Temporary restriction (1–30 days) |
| 🔴 **Severe** | Hate speech, credible threats, exploitation | Permanent ban |
| ⚫ **Critical** | Child safety, illegal content, platform attacks | Immediate ban + law enforcement referral |

### Repeat Violations

Accounts with repeated violations face escalating enforcement, including permanent termination regardless of the severity of individual incidents.

## 15. Updates to These Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated to reflect changes in the platform, legal requirements, or community standards.

**When we update these guidelines:**
- We will post a notice on the platform
- For significant changes, we will notify users by email
- Changes take effect 30 days after notification (except for legal or safety requirements)
- Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance
