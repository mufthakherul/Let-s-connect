## 1. Video Platform Overview

The Milonexa video platform is accessible via **Videos** in the main navigation or at **/videos**.

### What You'll Find

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|

## 2. Watching Videos

### Video Player Controls

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| ▶️ / ⏸ | Play / Pause |
| ⏩ | Skip forward 10 seconds |
| ⏪ | Skip back 10 seconds |
| 🔊 | Volume control |
| ⚙️ | Quality, speed, captions |
| ⛶ | Fullscreen |
| 🖼️ | Picture-in-picture mode |
| ↕️ | Mini player (keep watching while browsing) |

### Keyboard Shortcuts (Desktop)

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `Space` | Play / Pause |
| `←` / `→` | Skip back/forward 5 seconds |
| `↑` / `↓` | Volume up/down |
| `F` | Toggle fullscreen |
| `M` | Mute/unmute |
| `C` | Toggle captions |
| `0–9` | Jump to percentage of video (e.g., 5 = 50%) |

### Video Quality

Click ⚙️ Settings → Quality to choose:
- **Auto** (recommended — adapts to your connection)
- 1080p HD
- 720p
- 480p
- 360p

### Closed Captions (CC)

Videos may have auto-generated or manually uploaded captions.
1. Click the **CC** button in the player.
2. Choose language.

Auto-generated captions are available for most videos in supported languages.

### Playback Speed

Click ⚙️ → **Speed** to adjust:
- 0.25× — 0.5× — 0.75× — **1× (Normal)** — 1.25× — 1.5× — 1.75× — 2×

## 3. Uploading Videos

### Requirements

| Spec | Limit |
|------|-------|
| **Max file size** | 100 MB (free) / 1 GB (premium) |
| **Max duration** | 10 minutes (free) / 2 hours (premium) |
| **Accepted formats** | MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI, MKV |
| **Recommended format** | MP4 / H.264 + AAC audio |
| **Recommended resolution** | 1080p (1920×1080) |

### Upload Steps

1. Click **+ Upload** in the Videos section, or use the compose box → 🎥 video.
2. Select your video file.
3. While it uploads, fill in:
   - **Title** (required)
   - **Description** (supports markdown)
   - **Tags** (up to 15 tags)
   - **Category**
   - **Thumbnail** (auto-generated or upload custom — 1280×720 recommended)
   - **Visibility** (Public / Friends / Private / Scheduled)
   - **Language**
   - **Location** (optional)
   - **Allow comments** (yes/no)
   - **Allow downloads** (yes/no)
4. Click **Publish** when upload is complete.

### Processing Time

After upload, the video is transcoded to multiple quality levels. This typically takes:
- Under 3 minutes for videos under 100 MB
- Up to 15 minutes for larger or longer videos

You'll receive a notification when your video is ready.

## 4. Video Settings & Privacy

### Visibility Options

| Option | Who Can Watch |
|--------|--------------|
| 🌍 **Public** | Anyone, including non-logged-in users |
| 👥 **Friends** | Only your mutual friends |
| 🔒 **Private** | Only you |
| 🔗 **Unlisted** | Anyone with the direct link (not listed in search or your profile) |
| ⏰ **Scheduled** | Published automatically at a future date/time |

### Comments Settings

- **Allow all comments** — anyone can comment (default)
- **Approved comments only** — you approve each comment before it's visible
- **Friends only** — only friends can comment
- **Disabled** — no comments

Change comments settings: **Video → Edit → Comments**.

## 5. Playlists

Organise videos into playlists — your own or videos from others.

### Create a Playlist

1. Go to **Videos → Playlists → + New Playlist**.
2. Name the playlist.
3. Add a description (optional).
4. Set visibility: Public, Private, or Unlisted.
5. Click **Create**.

### Add a Video to a Playlist

Method 1: From a video page
1. Click **+ Save** (or the playlist icon) below a video.
2. Select an existing playlist or create a new one.

Method 2: From the Playlists manager
1. Open your playlist.
2. Click **+ Add Videos**.
3. Search or browse to find videos to add.

### Auto-Play in Playlists

When watching a playlist, the next video auto-plays by default. Toggle this in the player: ⚙️ → **Autoplay → Off**.

## 6. Photo Gallery

Your photo gallery collects all images you've posted.

### Accessing Your Gallery

- **Profile → Photos** tab
- Or visit any user's profile → **Photos**

### Gallery Organisation

Photos are organised by:
- **Recent** — chronological
- **Albums** — grouped collections
- **Tagged Photos** — photos you've been tagged in

### Tagging Photos

When uploading a photo, you can tag other users:
1. Click on the photo.
2. Click **Tag People**.
3. Click on a person's face and search for their name.

Tagged users receive a notification and the photo appears in their tagged photos.

---

## 7. Albums

Group related photos into albums for easy sharing.

### Create an Album

1. **Profile → Photos → Albums → + Create Album**
2. Name the album.
3. Upload photos or add from existing uploads.
4. Set cover photo.
5. Set visibility.
6. Click **Create Album**.

### Shared Albums

You can collaborate on shared albums:
1. Open an album → ⋮ → **Invite Collaborators**.
2. Search and add friends.
3. Collaborators can add photos to the album.

## 8. Video Comments & Engagement

### Commenting on Videos

The comments section appears below the video player.
- Type in the comment box and press **Enter** to post.
- Click **Reply** on any comment for threaded replies.
- React to comments (👍 like, ❤️ heart, 😂 laugh).

### Liking Videos

Click the **👍 Like** button below the video. You can also use extended reactions (hover for options).

### Share a Video

- Click **Share** → **Copy Link** for a shareable URL
- Or share directly to your feed, a group, or via message

### Saving Videos

Click **🔖 Save** to add to your bookmarks or a playlist.

## 9. Downloading Content

### Download Your Own Videos

You can download videos you've uploaded:
1. Go to **Videos → Your Videos**.
2. Click ⋮ on a video → **Download**.
3. Choose quality.
4. Download starts.

### Downloading Others' Videos

Whether you can download someone else's video depends on:
- Whether the creator has enabled downloads for that video
- If enabled, a **Download** button appears in the player (⋮ → Download)

## 10. Media Storage

### Storage Quota

| Plan | Storage |
|------|---------|
| Free | 5 GB total |
| Premium | 50 GB total |

Storage includes all uploaded videos, photos, and files.

### Checking Your Usage

**Settings → Account → Storage Usage** shows:
- Total used
- Breakdown by type (Videos, Photos, Files)
- A list of your largest files

### Freeing Up Space

Delete videos, photos, or files you no longer need from:
- **Videos → Your Videos → ⋮ → Delete**
- **Profile → Photos → ⋮ → Delete**

Deleted media is permanently removed from storage within 30 days.
